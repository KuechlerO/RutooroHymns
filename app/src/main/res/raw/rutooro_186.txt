186
WAKUGONDEZE YESU AKUSEMEZE?
Original: Would you Live for Jesus?
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 149
1.
Wakugondeze obe wa Yes(u) akusemeze?
Wakugondeze okumuhondera?
Wakugondez(e) ayetweke emigugu yawe? (O)mujuni akukonyere.
Chorus
Amaanige garakukonyera, (e)sagama ye erakwogyah(o) ebibi;
Arakwijuza Okugonzakwe, Omucunguzi akukonyere.
2.    
Nogonza otunge obusinge mu mwoyo? Osobolege okumuhondera;
Nagonza ogume omu mikonoye? (O)mujuni akukonyere.
3.    
Wakugondez(e) akuhikye mu kuhumura?
Wakugondeze obe musinguzi?
Nukwo otebeze baingi bamanye Yesu, (O)mujun(i) akukonyere.
