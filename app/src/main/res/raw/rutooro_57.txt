57
EBIRO EBINDI BIHOIRE
Original: Another Six Days are Done
Composer: Lowell Mason
Key/Doh: B
Luganda: 81
1.   
Ebir(o) ebindi bihoire, Sabiiti yait(u) ehikire;
Mutuma gwange weyeze, Ha kiro ekya Mukama.
2.   
Ramya Yesu (o)w’embabazi, Ayatuhaire ekiro;
Ha yataire emigisa, Erukukira oburungi.
3.   
Ntusiima Yesu (O)mujuni, N’okusemererwa kwingi;
Atuhe okuhumura, Okw’ahumuz(a) abantu be.
4.   
Obusinge bwa Mukama, Nugw(o) mukwatirizo gwe;
Ogw’ekihumuro kiri, (E)ky’okusemererwa kwingi.
