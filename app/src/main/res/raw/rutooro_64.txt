64
MPA EKITABU EKIRUKWERA MUNO
Original: Give Me the Bible
Composer: E. Simon Lorenz
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 52
1. 
Ngonza ebyahandikirwe bya Yesu,
Byolek(a) omuhanda ajwahire;
Busaho ekitanga (o)kujwa kwabyo,
Byolek(a) Omucunguzi nk’okwali.
Chorus
Mpa ekitabu (e)kirukwera muno, 
Nyolekwege omuhanda gwawe;
Nterekerr(e) omu biragiro byawe,
(O)kuhikyaYesu (O)bwaligaruka.
2.
(O)bumb(a) omu obujun(e) ompe etaara,
Ekibi (o)bukireet(a) okutiina;
Mukam(a) onyoleke (e)kigambo kyawe,
N’omusana gwabyo nkurolege.
3.
Omulikireg(e) omuhanda gwange,
Ompe okwiruka ansi enu;
Onjwesez(e) etaara ey’omu Iguru,
Enyolek(e) omuhanda (o)murungi.
