195 
NYESIGA EMIRAGO
Original: Standing on the Promises
Composer: Rusell Kelso Carter
1.
Nyesiga emirago ya Yesu Kristo, Ndamukugizaga ebiro n’ebiro;
Ndazinaga ha bukurubwe wenka, Nyesiga emirago ya Isiitwe.
Chorus
Nye-si-ga-We, N’emirago y’Omujuni wange Yesu
Nye- si-ga-We, Nyesig(a) emirago ya Isiitwe.
2. 
Nyesig(e) emirago ey’amananu, Bizibu n’okutiina obu biija;
Kigambokye kiransinguzaga, Nyesig(a) emirago ya Isiitwe.
3. 
Nyesig(a) emirago eindukurora,
Habw’okuba neyeza n’esagama;
Nyesig(a) okwikara omu bugabebwe,
Nyasig(a) emirago ya Isiitwe.
4. 
Nysig(e) emirago ya Yesu Kristo,
(A)kanyolek(a) Okugonza ebiro byona;
Ansinguza n’amaani ag’Omwoyo, Nyesig(a) esagama ya Yesu.
