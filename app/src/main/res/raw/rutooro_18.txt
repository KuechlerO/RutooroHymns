18
IZOOBA OBULYAGWIRE
Original: At Even, Ere the sun was Set
Composer: G. Joseph
1.   
Izooba obulyagwire Yesu bakakuletera
Abantu abasaliirwe Boona okabatambira
2.   
Bakaija barwaire N’enfu z’emiringo nyingi,
Bakagenda behumuire, N’okusemererwa kwingi.
3.   
Ai Kristo Mujuni waitu Otukize hati bunu,
Binga obujune bwaitu Obututuntuza hanu.
4.   
Amaani gaawe golekwe Omuli itwe nkaira,
Yema hati omuliitwe, Otukize n’oganyira
