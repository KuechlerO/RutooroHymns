157
MU MAISO GA YESU
Original: To God be the Glory
Composer: W. H. Doane
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 3
1.
Mumaiso ga Yesu abaguzirwe, Kitebe kikooto abemeriire;
Bazina ngu Ayatwogeze wenka,
Ahabw(e) Ekitinisa (e)biro byoona.
Chorus
Tumuhe, tumuhe (e)kitinisa Yesu,
Tuzine, tuzine ens(i) esemererwe
Mwije hal(i) Isiitwe n’Omwana Yesu,
Ahabw(e) ekitinisa (e)biro byona.
2.
 Kiki ekyabogeze abaina ebibi?
Yesu akatwogya n’esagama ye;
Tuzina ngu ayatwogeze wenka,
Ahabw(e) ekitinisa (e)biro byoona.
3. 
Niky(o) ekibafora ab’omuhimbo,
Nikyo (e)kibatuza nibazina ngu;
Iwe Yesu wenka ayatwereze,
Ohabwe (e)kitinisa (e)biro byoona.
4. 
Bbo baali bajemu baitu hati,
Bahereza Yesu abamwesiga;
Bazina ngu Ayatwereze wenka,
Ahebwe (e)kitinisa (e)biro byona.
5. 
Yesu itwe (i)twena twakukyenerwe,
Baitu (o)katugonza obutwali babi;
Okatuganyira kandi okatwogya;
Ohabwe (e)kitinisa (e)biro byoona.
