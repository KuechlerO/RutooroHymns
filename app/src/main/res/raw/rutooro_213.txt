TAATA ONYIRIZE HAIHI NAIWE
Original: Closer to Thee My Father
Composer: J. H. Tenney
Luganda: 148
1.
Taata onyirize haihi naiwe, mu kifuba kyawe
Nkwata n’emikono yaawe, mpumurre omuliwe
Chorus
Nkwata n’okugonza kwawe
Nyiriz(a) iwe hooli
Ndeet(a) owawe, nyikale naiwe.
2.    
Taat(a) omujuni ndeeta hooli, Nyikalege naiwe,
Nkwata omu mikono yaawe njwahire okujeera.
3.    
Nyiriza n’amaani g’Omwoyo, Nukwo nkusisane,
Nyogya nsemere n’esagama okwera titiiti.
