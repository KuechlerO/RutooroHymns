8
NINKUSIIMA YESU KRISTO 
Original: O Happy Day!
Composer: E.F. Rimbault
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 36
1.   
Ninkusiima Yesu Kristo, Okuba Ruhanga wange;
Nikyo kinkyanganukisa, Nimgamb(a) okusemererwa
Chorus
Kansiime, kanasiime, Yesu (o)kunyogyah(o) ebibi
Akandoza ebyagonza, Antahye (o)mu bukama bwe;
Kansiime, kansiime, Yesu (o)kunyogyah(o) ebibi.
2.   
Kankukuratire hoona, Naiwe tolimbinga hooli;
Okanyeta n’okugonza, Nahurr(a) irak(a) erirungi.
3.   
Nkatwarwatwarwa eby’ensi, Hati ntebekiire hooli;
Kangumirege hali Iwe, Mukama onyina byona.
4.   
Ndarahiraga ndi Waawe, Kibege kizina kyange;
Mukama (o)bwoligaruka, Nyikalege hamu na Iwe.
