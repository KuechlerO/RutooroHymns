OMWOYO ARUKWERA OMUGABI W’OBWOMEEZI
Original: Come, Gracious Spirit Heavenly Done
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 42
1.    
Omwoyo Arukera, Omugabi w’obwomeezi;
Tukwesengeriize ija, (O)mwoyo sirimuka.
2.    
Otwoleke n’koku tuli, Tusaliriirwe ebibi;
Otwoleke ebya Yesu, Tumusiime (o)kutujuna.
3.    
Twehaireyo hali Iwe, Magezi gaawe twesiga;
Tumalem(u) ebibi byaitu, Tujune twehaireyo.
4.    
(O)mubazi gwawe (o)bugusarra, (A)kahyo kaawe (o)bukasara;
(O)mufumu waitu twikirize, Otutambire itwe.
