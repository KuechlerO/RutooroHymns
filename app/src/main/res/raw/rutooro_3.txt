3
KATUKUGIZE RUHANGA
Original: We Praise Thee O God
Composer: J. J. Husband
Luganda: 2
1.
Katukugize Ruhanga waitu,
Ayafire hati ali omu iguru.
Chorus
Aleruya tukugize; Aleruya Yesu,
Aleruya tukugize; Tukiz(e) obundi.
2.
Katukugize Isiitwe n’omwoyo,
Ayatwolekere Omujuni waitu.
3.
Katukugize Omwana gw’entaama,
Ayayereeze ebibi byaitu byona.
4.
Ntukusaba tukirize kimu,
Omurro gwakege mu mituma yaitu.
