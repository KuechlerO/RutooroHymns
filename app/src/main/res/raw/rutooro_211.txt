211
AHAISANIZIBWE  
Original: Kozesa lraka lyekizina
Composer: Fanny Y. Crossby
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 3
1.
Ahaisanizibwe habw’ebirungi, Akatuha Omwanaawe Yesu Kristo,
Ayatucungwire obuyafire, Yakingur(a) orwigi hali abasiisi.
Chorus
Tumuhaisanize ensi yoona ehurre
Tumuhaisanize twijule omuhimbo
Tugende haluwe mu Yesu Kristo
Tumukugizege habw’ebirungi.
2.   
Okujunwa kwaitu babw’esagama, Hali buli muntu ain(e) omurago,
Omusiisi weena amwikiriza, Aganyirwa bwangu Yes(u) Omujuni.
3.   
Ebikuru bingi (e)byatukoliire, Nikyo tusemereriirwe Omwanawe,
Ebikuru muno n’okukiraho, Yesu waitu obu aliija okututwara.
