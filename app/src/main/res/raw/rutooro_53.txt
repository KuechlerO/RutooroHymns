53
MUGUME HABWA YESU
Original: Stand up! Stand up for Jesus
Composer: G. Duflield
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 100
1.   
Mugume habwa Yesu inywe abasaija be,
Murwane obulemu obw’obukama bwe,
Amahe ga Setani tigalisingura,
Amaani ga Mukama nugo gasobora.
2.   
Mugume habwa Yesu Muhurr(e) iraka lye,
Nayet(a) abantu boona okumuhondera,
Ebibi n’ebisuba tibiriikaraho, Yesu alibibinga ha kiro ky’empero.
3.   
Mugume habwa Yesu mutalyeyesiga,
Amaani gaanyu inywenka tigabagasira,
Empirima y’omwoyo nikyo (E)kitabu kye,
Engabu y’okusemba nukwo (O)kwikiriza.
4.  
Mugume habwa Yesu mutalituntura,
Ekiro kiri haihi ekyaraiziramu,
Mukama alituha abamukoliire,
Ekondo y’obwomeezi (o)bw’ebiro n’ebiro.
