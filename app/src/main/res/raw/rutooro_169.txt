169
GAMBIRA YESU 
Original: Are you Weary?
Composer: E. S. Lorenz
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 124
1.
Toin(a) amaani kandi olemeriirwe? Gambira Yesu, gambira Yesu;
Osaliirwe tonyin(a) omuhimbo, gambiraYesu wenka.
Chorus
Gambira Yesu, gambira Yesu, Og(u) akugonza muno;
Toina enganjani mugenzi waawe, gambira Yesu wenka.
2.
Ob(u) osangwa n’obujune bwingi, gambiraYesu, gambira Yesu;
Oin(e) ebibi otakayatwire, gambira Yesu Wenka.
3.
Okutiina n’ebizibu byensi? Gambira Yesu, gambira Yesu;
Otinire ebiraija nyenkya, gambira Yesu wenka.
4.
Okutiina n’okufa niibija, gambira Yesu, gambira Yesu;
Omuhimbo gw’okwija kwa Yesu, gambira Yesu wenka.
