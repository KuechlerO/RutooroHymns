101
ABARUKWERA MUROLE 
Original: Watch Ye Saints 
Composer: W. J. Kirkpatrick
Key/Doh: Db
Luganda: 160
1.   
Abarukwera murole, Iguru niritengeeta,
Muhembe etaara zaake, Murole Mukama naija.
Chorus
Allija! Yesu naija! Naija n’ekitinisa
Hamu N’abamaraika, Aliija! Yesu naija.
2.   
Teer(a) engwara orangirre, Obukwenda (o)bwokujunwa,
(O)bw’omufumu ow’engonzi, Aijwire (o)kuganyira,
3.   
(E)myehembo y’ens(i) erihwaho, Yesu (o)bwaliija kulema,
Obubi bw’ensi buhweho, Okuhikirra nukwija,
4.   
Amahanga nigahwerra, Ens(i) eijuire ekiniga,
Kristo nayanguha bwangu, (O)bunabbi nubuhikirra,
5.   
Dora (o)kunihira kwaitu, Ebibi bituganyirwe,
Kandi turatung(a) ekondo, Kusemererwa kwijwire.
