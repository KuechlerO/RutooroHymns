36
YESU MUJUNI OLI WANGE
Original: Blessed Assurance
Composer: Mrs. J. Fairchild Knapp
Key/Doh: Db
Luganda: 51
1.   
Yesu Mujuni oli wange, Kaniiwe Kitinisa kyange;
Kaniwe ayatuguzire; Yatwogya omu sagama ye.
Chorus
Ndamuziniraga Mujuni, Nimukugiza buli kiro;
Ndamuziniraga Mujuni, Nimukugiza buli kiro.
2.   
Kinsemeza okuba wawe, Kuror(a) okugonz(a) oku kwona;
Bamalaika be nibaija, Okunyoleka (o)kugonza kwe.
3    
Omuluwe nuho mpumurra, Nyin(a) okusemererwa kwingi;
Ninyehurra (o)mu mwoyo gwange, Ntair(e) okunihira (o)muluwe.
