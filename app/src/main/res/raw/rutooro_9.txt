TUGONZA OKUZINA HA YESU
Original: Redeemed! How I Love to Proclaim It
Composer: W. J. Kirkpatrick
Key/Doh: A
1.   
Tugonza (o)kuzina ha Yesu, (A)kancungura (O)buyafiire,
Akanjuna (o)mukugonzakwe, Ndiikarahamu Nauwe.
Chorus
Akanjuna, akanjuna (o)buyafire,
Akanjuna, ndiikara hamu Nauwe.
2.

3.

Kand(i) obunsemererwa nzina, Ha kugonza kwe okwingi.
4.

Andinda (o)mu rugendo rwangege, Ansemeza omutuma.
