YESU MUKAMA OHURRE
Original: Lord in the Morning
Composer: A. William
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 89
1.   
Yesu Mukama ohurre, Okusaba kwange;
Ninsab(a) omu Ibara lyawe, Ninkwet(a) onkonyere.
2.   
Omwoyo waw(e) ankonyere, (O)mumihanda yaawe;
Semeza byon(a) ebinkora, Nâ€™ebintekereza.
3.   
Ruhanga (O)muhikiriire, Onoba obubi;
(O)mu mbabazi zaawe zonka, Nkwiriire haihi.
4.   
Abatiin(a) Ibara lyawe, Olibasinguza;
Mukama engabu yaitu, Tukwesiga Iwe.
