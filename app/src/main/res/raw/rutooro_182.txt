182
HAROHO ORWIGI ORUKINGWIRE
Original: There is a Gate a Jar
Composer:  S. J. Vail
Luganda: 110
1.
Orwigi rukingwirweho, nuho nkurorra Yesu;
Kugonzakwe kwamahano, akehayo habwange.
Chorus
Kakugonza kwamahano, rukakingurwa habwange;
Rwange, rwange, rwakingurwa Yesu.
2.   
Orwigi oru rwa boona, (a)baserra Omujuni;
Abagaiga n’abanaku rwa buli muntu wena.
3.   
Omunyanzigwa w’amaani, baitu rukyakingwirwe;
(I)kiriz(a) otunge ekondo, wesig(e) okugonzakwe.
4.   
Butulisingur(a) itwena, turuge mu nsi embi;
Tulijwekwa (e)kondo (e)nungi turamukugizaaga.
