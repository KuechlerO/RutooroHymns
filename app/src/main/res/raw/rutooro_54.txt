54
AHAKUBANZA YESU
Original: The Sabbath of God
1.    
Ahakubanza Yesu akataho Sabiiti
Akagisororamu, kandi yagiha (a)bantu.
Chorus
Twasemererwa, Twasemererwa,
Twasemererw(a) ekiro Kya Sabbiti yaYesu.
2.    
Akakor(a) emirimo omu biro mukaaga,
Nubwo yah(a) omugisa Ekiro kya musanju.
3.    
(A) bantu bakahingisa Nibaremya ha Sande,
(O)mu Baibul(i) erukwera, Busamu kuhinduka
4.    
Abantu bamanyire (O)muntu akahingisa,
Baitu (o)mu Kigambo kye Busamu kuhinduka.
5.
(O)mu kusoma nindora Sabbiti eri emu,
Hati ningonza muno (O)kukwat(a) ekiro eki.
