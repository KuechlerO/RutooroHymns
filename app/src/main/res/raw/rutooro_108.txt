TULI BASERUKALE BA KRISTO YESU
1.   
Tuli baserukale ba KristoYesu, Nuwe manzi okutujuna;
Turamuhonderaga omu bulemu, Omu maani ge turarwana.
2.   
Tukaraganiza okuba besigwa, Ntuhurra engabwa yaitu;
Katuramage hali abanyanzigwa, Ntugonza bagenzi baitu.
3.   
Obuturajwarag(a) eby’okurwanisa, Ntusaba ntwerinda bwona,
Emyoyo y’omubi tetutinisa, Turagisinguraga yoona.
4.   
Omu maani ga Yesu tulisingura, Tuligum(a) okuhika ha mpero;
Tulihabwa empeera, tulihumura, Na Ruhanga ebiro n’ebiro.
