84
OMWOYO ARUKWERA 
Original: Holy Spirit Faithfiul Guide
Composer: M.M. Wells
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 41
1. 
Omwoyo Arukwera, Omwebembezi waitu,
Tukwate ha mukono, ltwena abagenzi;
Abanyin(a) obujune, Tuhurrege butoosa,
Iraka erirungi, Muije ndabahikya.
2. 
Omwoyo Arukwera, Atukonyera byona,
Tatuleka (o)mu kabi, Ak’omwirima Gw’ensi;
Tumaleh(o) obujune, Obuturahurraga,
Iraka erirungi, Muije ndahahikya.
3. 
(O)rugendo (o)burulihwa, ntukulindirra Iwe,
Eby’ensi (o)bubirihwa, (A) mabara ogatwaire;
Turabye (o)mu mihito, N’esagama ya Yesu,
Iraka erirungi, Muije ndabahikya.
