63
OMU KIGAMBO KYA MUKAMA TUSOMA
Original: Sure Word of Prophecy
1.   
(O)mu kigambo kya Mukama tusoma,
(O)rufumo (O)rw’amahano rw’embabazi;
Yesu ha musalab(a) akatufera,
Nuwe yatugondeze.
Chorus
Agonza twije haali, Agonza twije baali,
Tukwesengeriize okutujuna, Niiwe watugondeze.
2.   
Inywe ihe rya Yesu muhondere,
Murorre hali Yesu n’amaani ge;
W’obusobozi bwona (o)kutujuna, Nuwe yatugondeze.
3.   
Yesu nakweta hati omuhurre,
(O)mu kigambo kya Yesu titubiihwa;
Mukama w’amananu, mwebembezi,
Tumuhondere itwena.
