72
TEERA ENGOMA OMULINZI  
Original: Watchman Blow the Gospel Trumpet
Composer: William J. K.
Key/Doh: B
Luganda: 64
1.   
Teer(a) engoma omulinzi, Habura buli muntu;
Abarahurra engoma, Begarukemu bwango.
Chorus
Teer(a) engoma omulinzi, (A)bantu boona bahurre;
Yesu nukwo agonz(a) ati, Habur(a) abantu boona.
2.   
Teera ha buli kasozi, Na buli hantu hoona;
Nubwo okujunwa kwiije, (O)mu bizinga by’enyanja.
3.   
Omu mbuga n’omu byaro, N’omu bibira hoona;
Bahembemu (e)taara zaake, Mukama naija bwango.
4.   
Ababoherwe Setani, Batung(e) okulekerwa;
Omucunguzi natweta, Natweta twije hali.
