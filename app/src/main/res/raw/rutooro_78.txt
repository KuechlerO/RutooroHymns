78
IBARA LYA YESU OLIMANYIRE?
Original: The Name of Jesus
1.
Ibara lya Yesu olimanyire? Ruhanga akaliha;
Nagonza okujun(a) abantu boona, Kandi nagonza itwe.
Chorus
Ruhanga atuhair(e) okujunwa, Omw’ibara lya Yesu;
Busaho ibara erindi mali, Erijuna itwena.
2.
Ibara lya Yesu niryangasana, Nk(e) izooba butoosa;
Kandi omu Ibara linu mali, Tulisingura byona.
3.
Om(u) Ibara linu nubwo businge, Hali abantu boona;
Omu kufa kandi n’omu bwomeezi, Litulinda itwena.
