OBUNTUNGA OBUSINGE 
Original: When Peace like a River
Composer: William J. Kirkpatrick
Key/Doh: Db
Luganda: 127
1.   
(O)buntung(a) obusinge (o)mu mutuma gwange,
Rundi obumba (a) mu naku,
Onyegeseege okwikirizaaga, (O)mu mulingo gwona hontaire.
Chorus
Businge Businge, Businge Bwa Yesu. Mukama ningonz(a) obusinge.
2.   
Setani obwandetera ebyohyo bye, (O)mukasum(i) ako nihire,
Yesu (o)kwarora obugara bwange,
Kandi (o)bwaba na nyowe bwona.
3.   
Ekigambo kinu nikyo nyesigire, Ngu ebibi byange byona,
Akabibamba ha musalaba gwe, Mukama Yes(u) akugizibwe.
4.   
Mukama ayanguhyeho ekiro kye, Ekirisukura byona,
(O)kunihira kulihikirr(a) aizire, Nâ€™amaiso gange ndimurora.
