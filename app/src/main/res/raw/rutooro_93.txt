93
TULITUNGA OBUSINGE
Original: There is a Place of Quiet Rest
Composer: C. Boyd McAfee
1.   
Tulitunga obusinge, Haihi na Ruhanga,
Tihalibay(o) okusiisa, Haihi na Ruhanga.
Yesu Omucunguzi, Otutwale itwe,
Abakulindiriire, hali Isiitwe
2.   
Ekiikar(o) ekisemiire, Haihi na Ruhanga,
Hatuliba n’Omujuni, Haihi na Ruhanga.
3.   
Enkeeto tulizituurwa, Haihi na Ruhanga,
Oku h’okusemererwa, Haihi na Ruhanga.
