96
OMUJUNI NDI MURORA
Original: Face to Face
Composer: G. Colfax Tullar
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 175
1.   
Omujuni ndimurora, Ndisemererwa muno;
Obundimurora ogu, Yesu ayanferiire.
Chorus
Ndimurora, ndimurora, (O)bwalizira ha bicu;
Buli riiso lyona lyona, Lirirora Mukama.
2.   
Hati tindukumurora, Habw’omwirima gw’ensi;
Baitu ekiro nikiija, Ndimurora nk’okwali.
3.   
Tulyetendiga mahano, (O)muhito guhoireho!
Ensobi zisemeziibwe, Tuliba (o)mu businge.
4.   
Tulisemererwa muno, (O)butulibaza nawe,
Nituhurra iraka lye, Omucunguzi waitu.
