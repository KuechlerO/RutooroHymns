183
HAIHI N’OMUSALABA 
Original: Jesus Keep Me Near the Cross
Composer:  W. H. Doane
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 120
1.
Haihi n’omusalaba, haroho amaizi,
Agakwogya ebibi, onyirize haihi.
Chorus
Kuhikya (o)bwalija, ha musalaba gwe,
Nuho nyenyumiriza, nimpumurra hali.
2.   
Haihi n’omusalaba, nsangaho obusinge,
Omusana gwa Yesu, nigunyetoroora.
3.   
Nsangaho Yesu wange, nimuro wena,
Nangarramu amaani, nimpumurra hali.
4.   
Ndayesigaga Yesu, ndaikaranga nawe,
Nuwe wenka (a)nsemeze, ampikye mber(e) ali.
