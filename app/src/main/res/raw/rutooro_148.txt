148
MUJUNI NYEGESAGA
Original: Saviour Teach Me Day by Day 
Composer: A. L. Butler
1.
Mujuni nyegesaga, Ngonzege okuhurra;
Tinyina (e)kinsobora, (O)kugonza (a)yangondeze;
Nyowe tindukwemara, Ndi wawe kwo Mukama;
Ndema ondinde njunwe, Onyihe omu bibi.
2.
Nfora Mwana wengonzi! Ngenderege (o)muliiwe;
Nkuhereze bwoona, Iwe ayangondeze kwo;
Nkolege ebyogonza, Nukwo kibe Mukama;
Nzoke n’omuniiniiro, Mbe n’okukugonza.
3.
Enaku zawe nyingi, Okazigumira kwo;
Omwana wa Ruhanga, Okagumira byona;
Nyegesa nkuhondere, Mpa embabazi zawe;
Nyegesa nkugonzege, Iwe (a)yangondeze kwo.
4.
Ntwarwe (o)kugonza kwawe, Mpurrege ebyogonza;
(E)ngonzi ezitazooka, Ngonze eyangondeze;
Nyowe (o)muceke (e)ndoma, Tinyine Ekinserra;
(O)bundikurora nzine, lw(e) ayangondeze kwo
