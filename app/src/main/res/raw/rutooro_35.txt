35
BWONA GUMIRA HALI RUHANGA
Original: Firmly Stand for God
Composer: William H. Doane
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 68
1.   
Bwona gumira hali Ruhanga. N’obwaba oli (o)mu kabi kamgi;
Nuwe (o)mwandara gwawe (o)gw’amaani, (o)mu buli kabi koona.
Chorus
Leka tugumirege ha Mwandara, Yesu (O)mwandara gwaitu;
Obutuguma nubwo tulyemerra, (O)mumaiso ge tusingwire.
2.   
Buli kasumi ba w’amananu, Ogumire omu kwikiriza;
Ogu Mwandara ogusinguza, Amahe goon(a) ag’omubi.
3.   
Bwona gumiraga ha mananu, Ogu alihikiriza byona;
Nuwe Mwandara ogw’obusinge, Alitusinguz(a) ensi.
