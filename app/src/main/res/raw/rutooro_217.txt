217
ARAKULINDAGA
Original: God will take Care of You
Composer: Civilla Durfee Martin
1.
Otatiina ebizibu obu biija, Ruhang(a) obu aroho,
Oikale omu kifubakye, arakulindaga.
Chorus
Ruhang(a) obu aroho, Buli kiro hantu hoona,
Arakulindaga, arakumarra byona.
2. 
Omutima gwaw(e) obugujwaha,
Ruhang(a) obu aroho,
Ebizibu bingi nibiija, arakulindaga.
3. 
Ebyetaago byawe arabimara,
Ruhanga obu aroho,
Naija kukuha eki ora musaba, arakulindaga.
4. 
Ebirengo byona nâ€™obubiija, arakulindaga,
Igamira we wenka akugumye,
Ruhanga obu aroho.
