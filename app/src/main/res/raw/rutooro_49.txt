49
INYWE MUIJE HALl YESU
Original: Come to the Savior Make no Delay
Composer: G. F. Root
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 98
1.
Inywe muije hali Yesu, Ali hagati omul(i) itwe;
Muhurre hati iraka lye, Nagamba ngu ija.
Chorus
Tumwebinge dor(a) atwesire, Natugonza Itw(e) abasiisi;
Akatufera ha musalaba, Okutucungura.
2.
Muije inywe abajwahire, Inywe (a)basiisi abalemiirwe;
Yesu arabatuur(a) enkeeto, Azetweke wenka.
3.
Abantu boona kisoboka, (O)kuganyirwa hati ebibi;
Abaana bange nimbagonza, Mwehonge hali nyowe.
