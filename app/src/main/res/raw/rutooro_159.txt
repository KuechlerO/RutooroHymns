159
ONYESIRE MUKAMA
Original: I Hear Thy Welcome Voice
Composer: L. Hartsough
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 142
1.
Onyesire Mukama nyow(e) okwija hooli;
Onsemeze n’esagema, y’omuhendo mwingi.
Chorus
Ninyija Yesu, ninyija hooli;
Onyogye onsemeze, n’esagama yawe.
2.   
Nyowe ndi mugara, ndi mubi ndi busa;
Anyizira timubinga Nikyo nyesigire.
3.   
Yes(u) ayanferiire, Nyowe Omusisi;
Okugonza kundesire, nyizire mber(e) oli.
4.   
Ruhanga Isiitwe, na Yesu Omujuni;
N’Omwoyo Arukwera, tusiime butoosa.
