206
NDIHIKA HA KYAMBU   
Original: We’ll Tarry by the Living Waters
Composer: J.P. Webster
Key/Doh: E
Luganda: 181
1.
Ndihika ha kyamb(u) ekirungi, habw’embabazize;
Isiitwe atulindiriire, okututangirra.
Chorus
Ndihika ha kyambu kyamaizi, ekirug(a) omu ntebe ye;
Isiitwe Ow’omu iguru, hamu n’Omwana Yesu.
2.    
Halibaho Okuhumura, Hal(i) abajwahire;
Ha kyambu kiri ekirungi, ekikwangasana.
3.    
Ija hal(i) amaizi agoomire, otunge amaani,
Osemererwe muli Yesu, okire Iroho.
