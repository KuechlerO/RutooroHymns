114
NCWERA OMUGAATI
Original: Break Thou the Bread of Life
Composer: W. F. Sherwin
Luganda: 192
1.   
Ncwera omugaati, Ai Mukama, Nk’ogu wacwire ha Nyanja ira;
Ninkuserr(a) omu byahandikirwe,
Nkonyer(a) Iwe Kigambo (e)kyomeezi.
2.   
Niiwe omugaati, Gw’obwomeezi, Ndumirwe enjara y’ekigambo;
(O)mpaho ndyeho, nyikale naiwe,
Onyegese (o)kugonz(a) amananu.
3.
Omubiri gwaw(e) okaguhayo, Kub(a) eby’okulya ebitahwaho;
(E)sagama yaawe ndagaano mpyaka,
Ekaseseka kwihah(o) ebibi.
4.    
Ohe omugisa Amananu, Iwe (a)g’otusukuliire hati;
(O)bungonz(a) okworoba ngumirirwa,
Yesu (O)mukonyezi onsinguze.
