191
NKUGONZA
Original: I Love Thee
Composer: John Al. Granade
Luganda: 223
1.
Nkugonza, nkugonza, nkugonzaYesu;
Mucunguzi wange, Ruhanga wange.
Ndaba ninkugonza, okimanyire;
Ebikorwa byange nibikigumya.
2.   
Kwegonza, Kwegonza, kakwamahano;
Ndengire, ndengire, ensi enungi.
Ningonza okwikara, hamu Na Yesu;
Nokusemererwa n’abamalaika.
3.   
Mucunguzi wange, ampa kwegonza;
(O)businge (O)bwomezi, Nokuhumura.
Kugonza kwe kwingi, Ndamusimaga;
Nomutima gwange, ndamukugiza.
4.   
Noha arukwingana na Iwe Yesu;
Oundazinahog(a) ebiro byona.
Leeka mukugize, Muzinirege;
Habw’okuba nyijwire Omuhimbo.
