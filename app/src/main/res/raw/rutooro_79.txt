79
ABAANA BA RUHANGA OW’AMAANI
Original: Always Cheerful
Luganda: 217
1.    
(A)baana ba Ruhanga (o)w’amaani, Muhurre okwaturagira,
Natugamba tusemererwe, Mukama atugonza.
Chorus
Mu mituma tunyin(a) obusinge, Tukuratir(a) omucunguzi;
Atuh(a) okusemererwa kwingi, Atulind(a) omu kabi.
2. 
Tukuratira Ruhanga, (O)mu rugend(o) atulinda mali;
Atunihiza omugisa, Katugende nawe.
3. 
Abato n’abakuru, Boona tukole n’obwekambi;
Tutajwaha ahonaaho, (O)kukonyera abantu.
