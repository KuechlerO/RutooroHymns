41
YESU NANGAMBIRA OKUNDI
Original: I Hear the Savior Say
Composer: E. M. Hall
Luganda: 19
1.    
Yesu nangambira, Okundi (o)muceke;
Wekambe (O)kusaba, Ndakuh(a) ebyosaba.
Chorus
Akandeherra, Ibanja lyona,
Yanganyira ebibi, Nuwe yanyereze.
2.    
Ai iwe Mukama, Oli ow’amaani;
Ha ndwara y’ekibi, Oku (o)mu mutuma.
3.    
Nyowe nimpamanga, Nuwe nyesigire;
Kankukuratire, Oranyebembera.
4.    
Haihi n’ekitebe, Ndiba nyemeriire;
Ndireet(a) eminyago, (O)mu maiso ga Yesu.
