LEMA OBWOMEEZI BWANGE 
Original: Live out Thy Life within Me
Composer:  S. Westy
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 147
1.
Lem(a) obwomezi bwange, Iwe Yesu Mukama,
Niwe magezi gange, mu byon(a) ebindema;
Yesu ikara nanye mu byon(a) ebinkora,
Mbe ky’okurorraho nyolekege Iwe.
2.   
Kambe Yekaru yawe, etalumu kibi,
Ekitinisa kyawe, kizooke nanyowe;
Eby’ensi kambireke, okuruga hati,
Mbe mwiru waawe mali nkole (e)by’ondagira.
3.   
Yesu nyowe nyeena kanyikale hooli,
Nkozesa eby’ogonza tinkyali ha bwange;
Buli kiro nyetaaga kunterekereza,
Ompabulege Taata mu mutima gwange.
4.    
Kambe omuculezi mu mutima gwange,
Ndagira eby’ogonza niwe ompanurrege;
Lem(a) obwomezi bwange Yesu Omujuni,
Niwe Magezi gange, mu byon(a) ebinkora.
