209
OTURAGE N’OBUSINGE  
Original: Lord Dismiss Us with Thy Blessing
Composer: John Fawcett
Key/Doh: E
1.
Oturage n’obusinge, otwijuze omuhimbo;
Kand(i) otuhe okugonza, tusiim(e) embabazi zawe;
Tuhe amaani, gatusinguz(e) esi enu.
2.   
Ohaisanizibwe bwona habw’ekigambo kyawe;
Lek(a) obwomeezi obuhyaka, bwikale omuliitwe
Tuikalege abeesigwa, okuhikya obu oliija.
