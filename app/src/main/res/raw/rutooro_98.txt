TIMANYIRE OKWIJA KWA YESU  
Original: We know Not the Time when He Cometh
Composer: W. H. Pontius
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 174
1.
Timanyir(e) okwija kwa Yesu, Saaha emu rundi mukaaga;
Rundi ekiro omu itumbi, Rundi nyenkyakara muno;
Natugamba twetekanize, Tutaraaz(a) etaara zaitu;
Habw’okub(a) obwaraaba naija, Tumutangirre Mukama.
Chorus
Tusabe muno N’okumulindirra,
Mali tukiise ObuturalindaYesu
Tusabe muno N’okumulindirra,
Tusabe muno N’okumulindirra,
Mali tukiise N’okumusaba, Obuturalinda Yesu.
2.   
Okugonza kwe kwa mahano, Akatujuna itwena;
Akasiga entebe y’obukama, Yaija (a)kutufera;
Ebiro byon(a) asemererwa, Obwarora abantu be;
Aba boon(a) abamunihira, Okumurora Mukama.
3.   
Yesu Omucunguzi wange, Omanyire ninkugonza;
Ninihira okukurora, Obworaaba n’ogaruka;
Balicwer(a) omusango abantu, Abandi balliruka;
Baitu nyowe ndisemererwa, Habw’okulindirra Yesu.
