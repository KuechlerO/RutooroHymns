103
NTEBEZA IWE OMULINZI 
Original: Watchman Tell Me
Composer: William B. Bradbury
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 74
1.   
Ntebeza Iwe (o)mulinzi obwire buli buta?
Nyolek(a) obw’okurorraho (o)kutuiriire Sayuni,
Omugenzi sisimuka, Oraramire iguru;
Wefungiz(e) eby’okujwara, Emamba esazire.
2.
Omulinzi (o)musan(a) ogu gukujwera hoogenda;
(O)bwokurorraho bwoleka Okwija kuli haihi.
Engwara obwerigamba okusisimur(a) abafu,
Abe boona abaafire, bajwekwe obutafa.
3.
Omusana gwiija bwangu, Sabbiti ya Jubiri;
Boona bagambira hamu Obukama bwe bwiije.
Nukwo nu ab’oruganda Kanaani yaitu niija,
Yerusalemu (O)ruhyaka nirwangasana muno.
4.
Omu rubuga rw’ezaabu Omukama asingire;
Hakitebe ky’obukama (O)mulemi w’ensi zoona.
Businge ha mbaju zoona, Itaka lijwir(e) orwezo,
Hoona ha misa y’ ebyambu haculiire kurungi.
5.
Omulinzi tuiriire Ensi enungi muno;
Semererwa otafubeerra ensi n’ekyanganura.
Muhurr(a) ekizina kyabu, Abacungwirwe Mukama,
Omalirre otafeerwa (o)kuba mu bacungwirwe.
