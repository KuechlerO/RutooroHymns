171
AMAANI GA YESU GAKWEREZE?
Original: Have you been to Jesus?
Composer: E. A. Hoffman
Luganda: 177
1.
Amaani ga Yesu gakwereze? Oyogere (o)mu sagamaye?
Iwe noyesiga embabazize, Kandi onaab(e) omu sagamaye.
Chorus
Oyoge, mu sagama, (E)y’omwana gw’entaama Yesu;
(E)by’okujwara byawe byeriire kimu?
Oyoge (o)mu sagamaye yonka.
2.   
Oikara na Yesu buli kiro? Esagamaye ekwereze?
Ohumuliire omul(i ) ogu ayafire, Oyoge omu sagama ye.
3.   
Obu aliija olib(a) omwikiriize? Oganyiirw(e) omu sagamanye?
Iwe kandi oliba osemeriirwe, habw’okwog(a) omu sagamaye.
4.   
Jura bwangu ebibi byawe byona, Oyoge (o)mu sagama ya Yesu,
Mulindirre otakanabire, Yanguha onabe (o)mu sagamaye.
