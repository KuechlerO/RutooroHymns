13
RUHANGA NTUKUKUGIZA
Original: New Every Morning
Composer: Samuel Wabbe
Luganda: 88
1.
Ruhanga ntukukugiza Habw’okutulind(a) ekiro;
Tuhairw(e) okugwijagira, Tuhumuziibwe oturo.
2.   
Otugaruirem(u) amani Ababaire bajwahire;
Kandi otuimukize, Tukinyina obwomeezi.
3.   
Abantu baingi bafire, Obutubair(e) omu turo;
Baitu itwe tulinzirwe, Okutatwarwa orufu.
4.   
Nahati tukusabire, Otuhe okukwoleka;
Nk’oku kituhikiriire, Itwe okukusiimaga.
