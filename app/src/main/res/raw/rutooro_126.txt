NOOHA AYAHANGIRE EBYAKYO
Original: Who Made the Flowers
1.   
Nooha ayahangir(e) ebyakyo, Ngambira manyire;
Ruhanga nuwe yabihangire, Nikyo mugondeza.
2.   
Nooha ayahangir(e) emiti, Ntebeza, Manyire;
Mukama nuwe yagihangire, Nikyo mugondeza.
3.   
Nooha ayahangir(e) ebicu, Ntebeza, Manyire;
Mukama nuwe yabihangire, Nikyo mugondeza.
4.   
Nooh(a) anyakutuh(a) omweru, Ntebeza, Manyire;
Mukama nuw(e) atuh(a) omweru, Nikyo mugondeza.
