22
ENGANJANI YAITU YESU   
Original: What a Friend We Have in Jesus!
Composer: C. C. Converse
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 130
1.   
Enganjani yaitu Yesu, Nuwe atwetwekera,
Obujune bwaitu bwoona, Atuh(a) okuhumura;
(O)munsi tuli (o)mu bujune, Tutalibanizibwa,
Yesu niiwe Omujuni, (E)nganjani y’abasiisi.
2.   
Enganjani yaitu Yesu, Anyin(a) maani maingi,
Nukwo nu ab’oruganda, Tuleke Okutiina;
Tumwesige atulinda, Tatulek(a) omu kabi,
Nuwe omulinzi waitu, (E)ngajani y’abasiisi.
3.   
Enganjani yaitu Yesu, Atwebember(a) itwena,
Omumwirima kasira, Nuwe Musana gwaitu;
Tulekege okugaya, Omugisa gwa Yesu,
Katukugizeege Ogu, (E)nganjani y’abasiisi.
