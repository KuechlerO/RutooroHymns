HURRA IRAKA LY’OMUJUNI
Original: Hark the Voice of Jesus
Composer:  D. March
Key/Doh: D
1.
Hurr(a) Iraka ly’Omujuni, aratebeza n’oha?
Abantu balindiriire, arabaihayo n’oha?
Yesu nayeta n’amaani, arakuh(a) ekisembo,
N’oha ahonaho aragamba, Taata ntuma ndagenda?
2.   
Nobu otambuke enyanja, kuserra abakafiri,
Noija kubasanga haihi, haihi n’omuka owanyu;
Nobu otaabe nk’amalaika, rundi nka Paulo,
Obaze ha burungibwe, (o)kuyafiire ha bwaitu.
3.   
Nobwotabe omulinzi, kwemerra ha Sayuni,
Kubolek(a) omuhandagwe, (o)bwomeezi n’obusinge;
Kusaba n’itungo lyawe, noija (o)kuhikiriza,
Obwesigwa nk’obw’Aroni, kusagika Nabbiwe.
4.   
Abantu bahwerekerra, Mukama akwesire,
Otalengaho kugamba Nyowe tinkusobora
Kora byona n’obumanzi, kandi n’osemererwa;
Bw’orahurra Irakalye, Gamba ndi han(u) ontume
