164
MWANYAKI OKUTAAHA? 
Original: How far from Home?
Composer: A. Rebekah S.
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 158
1.
Mwanyaki okutaah(a) owaitu? Iw(e) omulinzi ngambira;
Obwire okukya nibwira, (O)mwirima gugende.
Oculeere, oyanguhe, Gumy(a) okwikiriza kwawe;
Kuhikya ohikire nseeri, Okwikara bwona.
2.   
Nkamukaguza omurwani; yangambira ebya Yesu:
Nyowe ndarwana n’amaani, guma omutima,
Humura gumusiriza, korra Yesu okimanye,
Empeera yaawe y’amaani, obw’olisingura.
3.  
Nkongera namukukaguza, ens(i) enyanja, izooba byagamba;
Obwire haliyo buke, tutaah(e) omw’iguru,
Humur(a) omwoyo, obagamba, (e)byoleka okwija kabingi,
Ensi yoona nerindiirra, kugamba (o)kw’engwara.
