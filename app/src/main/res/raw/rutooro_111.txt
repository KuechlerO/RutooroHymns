111
OBUNDORA OMUSALABA
Original: When I survey the Wondrous Cross
Composer: E. S.Widden
Luganda: 63
1.   
Obundor(a) Omusalaba, Mukama w’ekitinisa,
Ha yanferiire ningaya, Eby’ensi ebitagasa.
2.   
Obunyijuk(a) obujune, N’okusalizibwa kwona,
N’ebi byon(a) ebiyaboine, Habwange habw’okunjuna.
3.   
Nindamya n’okuhunirra, Ayankolir(e) ebi byona,
Nyehongere nimbundaara, Hal(i) okugonza kwa Yesu.
4.   
Tinyin(a) eky’okwenyumiza, Rundi okufa kwaYesu;
Ebindi tibisobora, (O)kunyihaho (e)bibi byange.
5.   
Hati eky’orukugonza, Nikyo ndukuhayo hooli;
Okugonza kwange kwona, Nkulesere namber(e) oli.
