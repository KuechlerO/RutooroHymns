HABW’EMIGISA YE YOONA
Original: Praise God from whom all blessings
Composer: Louis Bourgeois
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 12
1.
Habw(a) emigisa ye yoona,
Ensi zoona zikugize;
Ab’omu Iguru baramye,
Isiitwe Omwana n’Omwoyo.
2.
Mukama Ruhanga waitu,
Itwe tukahangwa Iwe;
Niiwe watucunguire itwe,
Itwe tuli ntaama zaawe.
3.
Omu mulyango gw’Iguru,
Itwe tuirra haihi Naiwe;
Ohaisanizibweg(e) Iwe,
Twinamega namber(e) oli.
