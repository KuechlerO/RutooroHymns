166
ONYIRIZE HAIHI MUKAMA
Original: I am Thine, O Lord
Composer: W. H. Doane
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 144
1.
Ndi waawe Yesu, mpuliir(e) Iraka eryawe lyangambira;
(O)kugonza, kwonka okwikiriza, nukwo (o)nyirize haihi.
Chorus
Onyirize, haihi Mukama, Ha musalaba gwawe;
Onyirize haihi naiwe Mukama, N’omusalaba gwawe.
2.    
Ongumye Yesu nkukorre bwona, N’embabazi eza Isiitwe;
(O)bwomeezi bube n’okunihira, Nyowe nyena mbe wawe.
3.    
Nkwatw(a) Omuhimbo habw’obwire obu, (O)bumara naiwe Yesu;
Omu kusaba omu maiso gawe, Enganjani ounsiima.
