31
NINGONZA OMUCUNGUZI 
Original: I must have the Savior with Me
Composer: John R. Sweney
Luganda: 154
1.   
Ningonza Omucunguzi, Habw’obuceke bwange;
Nyikale omu maiso ge, Ankwate ha mukono.
Chorus
Mubyona ndagumaga, Harantwaraga hoona,
Tindihyema omu kabi, (O)bunyebemberwa Yesu.
2.   
Ningonza Omucunguzi, Nuw(e) ampa okusingura,
A limpikya n’obusinge, N’ iraka lye (e) rirungi.
3.   
Ningonza Omucunguzi, omu rugendo rwange;
(O)mu kuhiigwa kwange kwona, N’Omu byohyo by’omubi
4.   
Ningonza Omucunguzi, anyoleke (O)muhanda;
Ansinguze obulemu, Mpik(e) omu maka gaitu.
