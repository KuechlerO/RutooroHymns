30
TWARA IBARA LYA YESU  
Original: Take the Name of Jesus
Composer: W. H. Doane
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 28
1.   
Twara Ibara lya Yesu, Omwana w’obujune;
Lisemeza omutuma, Liitwale hoona hoona.
Chorus
Ibara lirungi, Litucungura mali;
Ibara lirungi, Tulinihira bwona.
2.   
Twara Ibara lya Yesu, nk’engabo ekulinda;
Ebyohyo (o)bubikwizira, Oyete Ibara lye.
3.   
Ly’omuhendo Ibara ly(e), Lisemez(a) omutuma;
Tukwasir(e) omu ngaro Ze, Itwe ntumuzinira.
4.   
Turamy(e) ibara lya Yesu, Ntwinama namber(a) ali;
Alitujweka ekondo, (o)rugendo (o)burulihwa.
