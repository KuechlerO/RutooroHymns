77
RANYA ENGOMA
Original: Sound the Battle Cry
Composer: W. F. Sherwin
1.
Rahy(a) engoma, dor(a) omunyanzigwa, Imuky (a) ebberu ya Yesu ;
Twar(a) eby’okurwanisa mugume,
Mwesig(e) ekigambo kye kyonka.
Chorus
Mwimuke inywena abarwani!
Mwetekanize duma hoona
Mugende nimugamba Hosana
Kristo nuwe Engabu yaitu.
2.
Tufooke ab’amaani turwane, Mumanye tulisingura;
Kwat(a) engabu imukya ebberu, Amananu galisingura.
3.
Ai Omuhangi otuhurre, Otukonyereg(e) ltwena;
(o)bulemu obubulihwa, Tujwekw(e) ekondo y’obwomeezi.
