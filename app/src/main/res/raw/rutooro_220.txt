TALl NYOWE LEKA YESU ARORWE 
Original:  Not I, But Christ
Composer: Fannie Eugenia Bolton
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 17
1.
Tali   Nyowe, leka Yesu Arowe,
Tali   Nyowe Kristo Ahulirwe,
Tali   Nyowe, Owokunyumizaaga,
Tali   Nyowe, Yesu Alemege.
2.    
Tali nyowe, Kristo Atugumyege,
Kristo Aturagaza, Amaziga,
Kristo wenka, Anyihah(o) omugugu,
Kristo wenka (a)nyihaho Okutiina.
3.    
Kristo   wenka,   Tibiri bigambo byange,
Kristo   wenka,   Teri emyepanko,
Kristo   wenka,   Tinyin(a) eby’okugamba,
Kristo   wenka,   Nyowe tinyegonza.
4.   
Tali nyowe, Kristo Agaba byona,
Tali nyowe, Ampa Obwomeezi,
Kristo wenka Omwoyo n’Omubiri,
Kristo wenka, Obuguuda bwange.
