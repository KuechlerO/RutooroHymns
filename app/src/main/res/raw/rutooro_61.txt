61
MUKAMA EBIRAGIRO BYE
Original: The Law of God
1.   
Mukama (e)biragiro bye, Nibimukugiza;
Bisobor(a) okunkonyera, Bireet(a) amagezi.
2.   
Engesoze (e)zirukwera, Zireet(a) omusana;
N’ebiragiro bye byona, Bireet(a) omusana.
3.   
Mwete Isiitwe Ruhanga, Niiwe (o)w’amazima;
Amananu ga Ruhanga, Yesu agooleka.
4.   
Kuragira kwe kurungi, Nukukir(a) ezabu;
Ningonza okukwerinda, Ebiro n’ebiro.
5.   
Onkonyere Omujuni, Okukuhereza;
(E)biragiro (o)bihandike, (O)mu nda mu mutuma.
