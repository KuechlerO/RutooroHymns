YESU OMWOROBI OMUCULEZI OMU MWOYO
Original: Jesus Meek and Humble of Heart
1.
Yesu omworobi omuculezi omu mwoyo,
Fora ogwange gube nkaiwe.
Yesu omworobi omuculezi omu mwoyo,
Fora ogwange gube nkogwawe.
2.   
Ai Yesu taaha omwange, Oikalemu onfol(e) owaawe.
Ai Yesu taaha omwange, Oikalemu onfol(e) owaawe.
3.   
Akasumi kalihika, Ai Yesu ontwale owawe.
Akasumi kalihika, Ai Yesu ontwal(e) owaawe.
