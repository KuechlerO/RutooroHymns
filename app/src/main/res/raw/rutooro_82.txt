82
NNDAKUHONDERAGA YESU 
Original: I will follow thee My Savior
Composer: J. L. Elginburg
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 138
1.
Ndakuhonderaga Yesu, Horantwaraga hoona;
Ebirambahoga byona, Niiwe ndayesigaga.
Chorus
Ndakuhonderaga Yesu, Iwe ayanferiire;
(O)bu balikuleka boona, Ndakukuratiraga.
2. 
(O)mu handa harum(u) amahwa, N’okutuntura kwingi;
Boon(a) obu balikuleka, Ndakukuratiraga.
3. 
(O)mu muhanda gw’omwirima, kandi mufunda muno;
(O)bumpurra iraka lyawe, Ndakukuratiraga.
4. 
Omu nsi ndi w’obujune, Baitu ontungisa;
Iw(e) akab(a) oli munaku, Ndakukuratiraga.
5. 
(O)bundihika Yorudani (O)kugenda okuzimu;
(O)mujuni ha wambandize, Ndakukuratiraga.
