50
OMULIISA OMURUNGI AKWESIRE 
Original: Lovingly, Tenderly Calling
Composer: W. A. Ogden
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 105
1.
Omuliis(a) Omurungi akwesire hati;
Ij(a) omwisibiko lye, Ohumurre haali;
Omu busigazi bwawe rundi ohwisire;
Wetebuke iraka lye, Yesu akwebembiire.
Chorus
Akwesire n’embabazi nyingi, Rahuka oij(e) ayahabire;
Iwe nk’omugenzi ogaruke, Nkwesire ozaahuke.
2.
Omuliis(a) Omurungi (a)kahayo (O)bwomeezi; Akwesire
nagamba, Mwana wange ija;
Rahuka otakagwire omundubatira;
Omu busigazi bwawe, rundi omu kuhwita.
3.
Otaikaraho ija emyaag(a) etakulya;
Erukwita entaama, Ento hamu n’enkuru;
Yesu (O)muliisa natweeta (O)mucunguzi waitu, Tutaah(e)
omwisibiko, hakyarumu omwanya.
