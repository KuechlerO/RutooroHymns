144
YESU MUKAMA OMUCUNGUZI
Original: Jesus, My Savior, to Bethlehem come
Composer: E. E. Hasty
Luganda: 167
1.
Yesu Mukama Omucunguzi, akaija (o)mu nsi kuserra ababi,
Ka kwamahano okugonzakwe, akaij(a )okunserra.
Akaij(a) okunserra, akaija okunserra,
Ka kwamahano okugonzakwe, Akaij(a) okunserra.
2.   
Yesu Mukama Omucunguzi, Sitani obwakaba ambohere,
Akancungura n’Esagamaye, Yesu akanfera.
Yesu kanfera, Yesu akanfera,
Akancungura n’Esagamaye, Yesu akanfera.
3.   
Yesu Mukama Omucunguzi,
(O)bunkaba ntali (o)mu muhandagwe,
Akanyeta n’okuganyirakwe, Yesu akanyeta.
Yesu akanyeta, Yesu akanyeta,
Akanyeta n’okuganyirakwe, Yesu akanyeta.
4.   
Yesu Mukama Omucunguzi,
(E)kigambo kinu kinkyanganukya,
Ndimurora naleetwa ha bicu, okuntwara owabu.
Okuntwar(a) owabu, Okuntwar(a) owabu,
Ndimurora naleetwa ha bicu okuntwar(a) owabu.
