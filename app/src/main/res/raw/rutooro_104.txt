104
OMULINZI HA MUHURUZI
Original: Watchman on the Walls of Zion
Composer: T. Hastings
Key/Doh: D
Luganda: 77
1.   
(O)mulinzi ha muhuruzi Y’orubuga Sayuni,
Hati (o)bwire buli buta? Emamba esazire?
Orozire enyunyuzi ya nyenkya? Enyunyuzi ya nyenkya tugiboine!
2.   
Abalinzi mumanyise (O)bw’okurorraho bwaitu,
(O)buturaaba tuli haihi okuhika ha musa;
Tuli haihi okuhika ha mwaro?
Tulengiire (a)maka gaitu (o)mw’iguru!
3.   
Turozire omusana, Enyunyuzi ya nyenkya,
Emamba nay(o) esazire neyangasana mali;
Semererwa okujunwa kwizire! Kyanganuka tuli haihi (o)kutaaha.
4    
Obunabbi nibwoleka Mutokere haiguru,
Habw’okuba tuli haihi, Okwambuka enyanja;
Murangirre kinu ha buli muntu! Okucungura kwaitu kuizire.
