5
RUHANGA W’OBUSOBOZI
1.
Ruhanga w’obusobozi kandi w’embabazi;
Afokere Ise nyowe nanyowe (o)mwana we.
2.
Busaho n’omu (o)mu bantu amwinganaingana;
Kugonza n’okuganyira nk’ogu Isenyowe.
3.
Akahayo Omwanawe okutucungura;
Ayiheyo abaana be barug(e) omu bibi.
4.
Bagarukeyo haluwa Oubayangire;
Batahe (o)mu kuhumura omu Iguru lye.
5.
Nanyowe nyin(a) Ibaralye ninyetwa wa Kristo;
Nkaragaana okuhurra Ruhang(a) Omu Yesu.
