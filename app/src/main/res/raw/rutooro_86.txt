86 
AI RUHANGA ISIITWE
Original: Build Their Home
1.    
Al Ruhang(a) Isiitwe Muhangi wa byoona,
Ir(a) ayateraniize Adamu na Hawa;
Abaana baawe banu obah(e) ebisembo,
Eby’okugonza kwawe, Obateranize.
2.    
Ai Mujuni waitu Ir(a) ayatahire,
Kana (e)ya Galiraya, okuh(a) omugisa;
Taah(a) obugenyi bunu, Oyez(e) enju yaabu,
Eijul(e) obusinge, Obutalihwaho.
3.    
Ai Mwoyo murungi, Hwezes(a) emituma,
Okuhikirra kwawe, N’okugonza kwona;
Hal(i) ekibi n’empaka, Balindwege iwe,
Olemege n’osemba, Emituma yaabu.
4.    
Yombeka enju yaabu, Isiitwe egume,
(O)kusemererwa kwabu, Kutabarugaho;
Yez(a) okugonza kwabu, Kugumisirize,
Baikal(e) omuliiwe, N’omutuma gumu.
