95
OMU RUBUGA RWA RUHANGA 
Original: Shall We Gather
Composer: Robert Lowry
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 176
1.   
(O)mu rubuga rwa Ruhanga, Nikirug(a) omu ntebe ye;
Haroh(o) ekyambu (e)kirungi, Ekireeta obwomeezi.
Chorus
Tulyesoroza ha kyambu, Ha kyembu Ky’obwomeezi mali,
Tugonza muno, tuhikeyo, Ha kyambu Ky’obwomeezi
2.   
Yesu Omuliisa waitu, (A)ratuterekerezaaga;
(O)mw’irisizo ly’okwesiima, Nambere ekyambu kiri.
3.   
Kyonka tutakahikire, Hali ekyambu kiri;
Enkeeto ozitutuule, Ez’obujune bwingi
4.  
Orugendo rwaitu runu, Rw’ebiro bike muno;
Itwe tituliikaraho, Okuterana nabo.
