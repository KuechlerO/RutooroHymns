KUGIZA MUKAMA
Original: Praise Him All Ye Little Children
1.   
Kugiza Mukama, Inywena abaana be,
Nuwe Kugonza, Nuwe Kugonza;
Kugiza Mukama, Inywe abaana be,
Nuwe Kugonza, Nuwe Kugonza.
2.   
Musiime Mukama, Inywena abaana be,
Nuwe Kugonza, Nuwe Kugonza;
Musiime Mukama, Iywena abaana be,
Nuwe Kugonza, Nuwe Kugonza.
3.   
Mugonze Mukama, Inywena abaana be
Nuwe Kugonza, Nuwe Kugonza;
Mugonze Mukama, Inywena abaana be,
Nuwe Kugouza, Nuwe Kugonza.
