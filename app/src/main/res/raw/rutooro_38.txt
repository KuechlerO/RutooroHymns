38
OBWOMEEZI BWANGE BUKYANGANUKA
Original: What A Fellowship
Composer: A. J. Showalter
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 43
1.   
(O)bwomeezi bwange bukyanganuka, Obunyesiga Yesu wenka;
Ntunga emigisa n’obusinge, Obunyesiga Yesu wenka.
Chorus
Rwana, rwana, Osingule habwa Yesu;
Rwana, rwana, Osingule omu maani ge.
2.   
Omuhanda gwe gukyanganukya, Habw’okuba atwebembera;
Omusana gwe gujwera boona, Habw’okuba atwebembera.
3.   
Busaho ekirantiinisaaga, Obunyesiga Yesu wenka;
Ntung(a) obusinge n’okunihira, Obunyesiga Yesu wenka.
