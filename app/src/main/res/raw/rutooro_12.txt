12
SISIMUKA MWOYO GWANGE  
Original: Awake My Soul
Composer: P. Doddridge
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 66
1.   
Sisimuka mwoyo gwange, Ondeter(e) amaani;
Yesu kutuha ekondo, Agonza tweheyo.
2.   
Iraka ly’Omucunguzi, Nayet(a) abaana be;
(O)kubagabira (e)bisembo, Ebyawe n’abandi.
3.   
Bakaiso baingi muno, Baror(a) ebyokora;
Byona ebya ira bireke: Genda omu maiso.
4.   
Yesu omurangi waitu, (O)murugendo rwona;
Otusinguze itwena, Tuhike haliiwe.
