TINGONZA KWESEMEZA N’EBIRUNGI BY’ENSI
Original: Is my Name Written There?
Composer: Frank M. Davis
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 170
1.   
Tingonza kwesemeza N’ebirungi by’ensi,
Ngonza eby’omu Iguru n’okuhika hooli;
(O)mu kitabu kyaYesu Harum(u) amabara,
Ntebeza ai Mukama, Harumu eryange?
Chorus
Mu kitabu omu harum(u) amabara,
Omanyise Mukama, Harumu eryange?
2.   
Ebibi byange biingi bindemeera muno,
Baitu Esagama teremwa ekibi;
Yesu okanihiza (o)mu Byahandikirwe,
Ebibi byaitu byona (o)kubyereza kimu.
3.   
Orubuga rwa Yesu (a)karutekaniza,
N’ekitinisa kingi nikimulikana;
Tihataaham(u) ekibi, kusiis(a) abaana be,
Haroho abalinzi, Okampandikamu?
