55
TUSEMERERWA OMU MWOYO
Original: Sweet Sabbath School
Composer: W. H. Doano
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 83
1.    
Tusemererw(a) omu mwoyo, Obutuhikaho;
Ha kiro ekya Sabiiti, Nturamya Yesu.
Chorus
Sabiiti yaYesu, Y’okusemererwa;
Ha kiro ekya Sabiiti, Turamya Ruhanga.
2.    
Endwara z’omwoyo zoona, Nuwe azitamba;
Nuho hali obusinge, N’oburungi bwona.
3.
Ndaikara na Mukama, Nimpurr(a) iraka lye;
Nuw(e) ampa okumugonza, N’omutuma gwona.
