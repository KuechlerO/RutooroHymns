94
HAROHO ENSI EKIRA ENU 
Original: There is a Land 
Composer: J.P. Webster
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 180
1.   
Haroho ensi ekir(a) enu, (A)baikiriza bagirora,
Isiitwe atulindiriire, Okutuh(a) ebiikaro byaitu.
Chorus
Mu kusemererwa, Tulirorangana itwena,
Mu kusemererwa, Tuliikara n’Omucunguzi.
2.   
(O)mu nsi egi turazinaga, (E)bizina by’okusemererwa,
Oku tihalibayo (o)kurra, Tuliikara omu businge.
3.   
Alituhikya namber(e) ali, Turasiimag(a) oburungi bwe,
(E)kisembo ekiyatuhaire, Kandi n’emigisa ye yoona.
