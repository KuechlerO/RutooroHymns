SABAGA NYENKYA KARA
Original: Whisper A Prayer in the Morning
1.
Sabaga nyenkya kara, Sabaga omu ihangwe;
N’omu kairirizi saba noija kuganyirwa.
2.    
Yes(u) akuhurra nyenkya, Akuhurra n’omu ihangwe
(O)mu kairirizi akuhurra, Otajwah(a) okusaba.
3.    
Yesu w’okwija nyenkya, Rundi omu ihangwe;
N’omu kairirizi naija, Tegereza bwona.
