AKWESIRE OGARUKE HALUWE
Original: Jesus is tenderly Calling Today 
Composer: G. C. Stebbins
Key/Doh: C
Luganda: 101
1.
Akwesir(e) ogaruke haluwe, Ija bwangu kiro kinu;
Kiki ekikwihire ha ngonzize, norugaho Omujuni?
Chorus
Ija hati, kanakweta;
Yesu nakweta, n’embabaziz(e) enyingi.
2. 
Yesu nayeta weena (a)jwahire, ija bwangu kiro kinu;
Muleter(e) Omugug(u) ohumule, takunage nakake.
3. 
Yesu alindiriir(e) ogaruke, (a)kulinzire kiro kinu;
Muleter(e) ebibi byawe byona, ija bwangu yanguha.
4. 
Akwesengeriiz(e) ohulirize, Irakalye kiro kinu;
Abamwikiriize b’omuhimbo, Imuka bwangu oije.
