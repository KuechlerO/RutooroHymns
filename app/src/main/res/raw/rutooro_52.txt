52
NKAHAYO OBWOMEEZI
Original: I Gave My Life for Thee
Composer: J. Edson White
Luganda: 97
1.    
Nkahay(o) obwomeezi Okukucungura,
(E)sagama y’omuhendo habwaw(e) ekaseeswa,
(O)kukucungura (o)mukufa ompaireki iwe?
(O)kukucungura (o)mu kufa ompaireki iwe?
2.   
Nkasig(a) ekitebe kyang(e) eky’obukama,
Owaitu omwiguru Omu kwangasana,
Ninyija omu bujune N’okubonabona,
Ninyija omu bujune N’okubonabona.
3.   
Nkasalizibwa muno Okutagambwa,
Ningumisiriz(a) ebi nukwo ocungurwe,
Ogumisiirize k(i) iwe Habw’ibara lyange?
Ogumisiirize k(i) iwe Habw’ibara lyange?
