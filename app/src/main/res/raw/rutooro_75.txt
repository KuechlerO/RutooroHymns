75
BARWANI BA YESU
Original: Onward Christian Soldiers!
Composer: A. S. Sullivan
Luganda: 65
1.   
Barwani ba Yesu mweteeg(e) enkoba,
Murole Mukama ayebembiire;
Tunyina engabwa akira boona,
Mukama natweta, tugende nawe.
Chorus
Barwani ba Yesu mweteeg(e) enkoba,
Murole Mukama ayebembiire.
2.   
Tugend(e) omu maiso, turwane muno,
Tubinge Setani n’ebisuba bye;
Twikiriize Yesu, engabwa yaitu, Hamu n’ekitabu ekirukwera.
3.   
Ibara lya Yesu ly’ekitinisa, (A)bantu ba Setani balyesereka;
Mube manzi mali, ab’oruganda,
(O)mu MukamaYesu tulisingura.
4.   
Mutebeze baingi, tugende nabo,
Nukwo balifooka (a)bantu ba Yesu;
Hat(i) ab’oruganda tugume muno,
Yesu (o)mw’ebembezi w’amaani maingi.
