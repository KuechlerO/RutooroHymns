71
OTETONGANIRRA    
Original: Ask Not to be Excused
Composer: F. Edson Belden
Key/Doh: E
Luganda: 57
1.   
Otetonganirra, omulimo mwingi;
Wehereyo kimu, Yes(u) akukozese;
Nembabazi ati ija ompereze, Wehereyo kimu, olikyanganuka.
Chorus
Ikiriza otetonganirra, Ikiriza wehereyo kimu;
(O)bwoyetonganirra, oragambirwa ngu,
Torukungonza Iwe, nanyowe nkwangire.
2.   
Otetonganirra, Yesu akwesire, Kiki oguniriza, Ikiriza hati;
(E)byokugesa bingi, Abakozi bake,
(I)kiriza kwehayo, Okumuhereza.
3.   
Otetonganirra, Kwikaraho kabi,
Nogadisa Yesu, ayakugondeze;
(O)mu kasumi kanu (E)mbabazi ikyaroho,
Nakutonganirra, ija oganyirwe.
