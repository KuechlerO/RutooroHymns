172
N’EMBABAZI ONGARUKEMU YESU 
Original: O Hear my Cry
Composer: William Howard Doane
Key/Doh: C
Luganda: 8
1.
N’embabazi ongarukemu Yesu, Ij(a) Omucunguzi;
Ninkwetaaga (o)mu bwomeezi bwange, Ija Omucunguzi.
Chorus
Ija Omujuni wange Yesu, ninkungira oku omu irungu;
Ndeta ongarr(e) omu baana baawe, ija Omucunguzi.
2.   
Tinyina bwirukiro Mukama, Ija Omucunguzi;
Amaiso gaawe gamp(a) obwomeezi, Ija Omucunguzi.
3.   
Omuhanda guraha njwahire, Ija Omucunguzi;
Engonzi zaawe zimp(a) obusinge, Ija Omucunguzi.
4.   
Tobinga weena anyakwegarukamu, ija Omucunguzi;
Ikiriza Okusaba kwange, Ija Omucunguzi.
