EMBABAZI EZIRUKUKIRA EKIBI
Original: Marvelous Grace of our Loving God 
Composer: D.B. Tonner
1.
(E)mbabaz(i) ez’omujuni Yesu, Nyingi okukir(a) omusango gwaitu;
Akazoolekera Gorogosa, Hayasesire esagamaye.
Chorus
(E)mbabazi (E)z’amahano, Embabazi zirituganyiza;
Embabazi eza Ruhanga, Tizigir(a) obugarukiro.
2.    
(E)kibi kireeta okutuntura, Kandi kireeta okuhwerekerra;
Kyonka okugonza okwa’amahano, Kunyoleka ha musalabagwe.
3.    
Omwirim(a) ogw’ekibi kamubi, Noh(a) asobora okugutwihaho;
Esagama eya Yesu Omujuni, Nesobora okukwereza kimu.
