16
SERRAGA MUKAMA OMU KUSABA 
Original: Take time to be Holy
Composer: G. C. Stebbins
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 134
1.   
Serraga Mukama omu kusaba;
Bazaaga na Yesu (o)mu Kigambo kye;
Konyer(a) abagara, gonzaaga boona,
Serrag(a) omugisa gwe obutoosa.
2.   
Serraga Ruhanga omu kusaba;
Ikara (o)mu nsita hamu na Yesu;
Habw’okumurora ofooke nkawe,
N’omu ngeso zoona yoleka Yesu.
3.   
Serrag(a) amaani ge omu kusaba;
Omutegereze akwebembere;
(O)mu kumererwa n’omu bujune,
Wesigege Yesu n’ekigambo kye.
4.   
Serrag(a) Omwoyo we omu kusaba
Otung(e) obusinge omu mutuma,
(O)bwoyebemberwa Omwoyo we wenka,
Akugondeza okumuhereza.
