67
MWEKAMBE HATI BUNU
Original: Work for the Night is Coming
Composer: L. Mason
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 59
1.   
Mwekambe hati bunu, Izooba liroho,
Omu rukyakya muno, Na ha nsonoki;
Omu ihangwe nalyo, Kandi nâ€™orwebagyo,
Baitu ekiro nikiija, Baliinuka.
2.   
Wekambe obwomeezi bwawe buroho,
(O)bwoba okiri muto, rund(i) okuzire;
Obwoba musigazi rundi mugurusi,
Baitu ekiro nikiija, Baliinuka.
3.   
Mwekambe inywe inywena omu mirimo,
Yesu nuwe wenka alibasinguza;
Inywe mutaliijwaha, Mutalifoberra,
Baitu ekiro nikiija, Baliinuka.
