23
TUKWESIGA IWE MUKAMA   
Original: Tis so Sweet to Trust
Composer: William J. Kirkpatrick
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 129
1.
Tukwesig(a) Iwe Mukama, Omu kigambo kyawe,
N’ebi ebiwanihiize, N’akanwa kaawe wenka.
Chorus
Ninkwesiga ai Yesu, N’Obusobozi bwawe,
Yesu Omurungi muno, Nkonyera nkwesigege.
2.   
Kirungi (o)kumwesigaga, Og(u) ayatuferiire,
Habw’okumuikiriza, Nyog(e) omu sagamaye.
3.   
Kirungi okumwesiga, Hatal(i) engeso zonka,
Baitu Ruhanga wenka, Nuw(e) aha ebirungi.
4.   
Nyongera okumwesiga, Yesu Omucunguzi,
Kandi oli wange mali, Ebiro byange byona.
