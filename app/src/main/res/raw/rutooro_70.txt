70
JUNA ABANTU ABALI OMU KUFA    
Original: Rescue the Perishing
Composer: W. H. Doane
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 56
1.   
Juna abantu abali (o)mu kufa, Basahule barug(e) omu kufa;
Ganyira abali omu kusiisa, Booleke okujuna kwa Yesu.
Chorus
Let(a) abantu abali mu kufa,
Bajunwe obw’embabazi za Yesu.
2.   
N’obubaraaba abo (a)bamugaya, Nagonza Okubajuna boona;
Nabeeta habw’okugonza okwingi, Bamwikirize abaganyire.
3.   
Byona Setani ebiyasiisire, Mukama naija (o)kubisemeza;
Habw’okugonza kwe n’embabazi ze, Arasemez(a) ebiyasiisire.
4.   
Wekambe okutebez(a) abasiisi, Mukama arakuha amaani;
Batahye omu Igana lya Yesu, Bamanyis(e) ayafire habwabu.
