YESU OMUGABO GWANGE
Original: Close to Thee
Luganda: 235
1.
Yesu Omugabo gwange, Iw(e) obwomeezi mali;
(O)mu rugendo rwange rwona, kandubatege Naiwe.
Chorus
Nyiriza mberoli, Nyiriza mber(e) oli;
Mu rugendo rwange rwona, kandubatege naiwe.
2.   
(O)kusemererwa okw’ensi n’okumanywa mbyangire;
N’obundabonabona nta, kandubatege naiwe.
3.   
Onyebemberege Yesu (O)mu bizibu eby’ensi enu;
Onkingurreho orwigi (O)rw’obwomeezi obutahwaho.
