219
YESU NAYETA N’EMBABAZI NYINGI  
Original: Softly and Tenderly
Composer: Will L. Thompson
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 103
1.
Yesu nayeta n’embabazi nyingi, nayeta (a)bantu boona
Ayemeriire akulindiire, Alinzir(e) iwe naanye.
Chorus
Mwije Mwije hati, Abajwahire muno, muno
Yesu nayeta n’embabazi (e)nyingi, atwesire itwena.
2. 
Yesu natwesengereza twangire, (a)yesengeriiz(e) itwena,
Habwaki n’oyang(a) okwetwa kwa Yesu, atwesire itwena
3. 
Akasumi k’embabazi kahwaho, noyenda kukingirwa,
(A)kasumi k’obujune kali haihi, katwizire mu bwangu.
4. 
Tekereeza (o)kunihiza kwe kwona kwanihiiz(a) iwe nanye,
Tukasiisa, kyonka yatuganyira, atugayir(e) itwena.
