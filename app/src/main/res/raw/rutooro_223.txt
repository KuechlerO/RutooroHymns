NYINE ENGANJANI YANGE
Original: I have a Friend so Precious
Composer: Mrs L. Shorey
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 61
1.
Nyine (e)nganjani Yange, Omurungi muno
Ampumuz(a) Omutima, kand(i) aba Mwesigwa;
Tinsobor(a) Okuhumura, Obwatamba haihi,
Nikyo twikarra hamu, Yesu naanye.
2. 
(O)bunjwaaha mpoir(e) amaani, Anyetegereza
Ahurr(a) okusaba kwange, kandi Ampuumuza;
Nyebemberw(a) Omusanagwe, Mpumurr(a) Omuluwe,
Nikyo ngendera nawe, Yesu naanye.
3. 
Ebibi nâ€™ebirungi, timbimusereka,
Mugambir(a) Ebinsemeza, neeb(i) ebinsaaliza;
Anyolek(a) Omuhandagwe, angamba ndengeho,
Tubaza hamu bwoona, Yesu naanye.
4. 
Amanyire nyegomba, Kutebez(a) Enjiri,
Anyekambisa ngende, Nduum(e) Ekigambokye;
Nkooku yafeeriir(e) ababi, Habwengonz(i) enyingi,
Nukwo Tukorra hamu, Yesu naanye.
