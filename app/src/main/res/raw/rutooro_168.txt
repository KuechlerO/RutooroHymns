168
HAROHO ENSORO EY’ESAGAMA 
Original: There is A Fountain
Composer: William Cowper
Key/Doh: C
Luganda: 13
1.
Haroh(o) ensoro ey’ esagama neruga omwa Yesu,
Abasiisi eiboogamu kuganyirw(a) ebibi.
Chorus
Ego mali ninkigumya Yesu akanfera,
Ha musalaba akeehayo njunw(e) ebibi byange.
2.
Omusuma akesiima, kuror(a) nsor(o) egi,
Nyowe nkawe nagyogamu Okwera titiiti.
3.
(E)sagama ey’omuhendo ijwirem(u) amaani,
Okuhikya abanabe basingwire ekibi.
4.
Kir(o) eki nagirozire obuwahutaire,
Engonzize nzizinaho kuhikya ha kufa.
5.
Ninkigumya(a) onyahuliire nyowe omusiisi,
Ekisembo ekya busa ekondo ey’ezabbu.
6.
N’okwebundaaza ndizina aburungi bwawe,
N’orulimi orw’ekibi ha kiro eky’okwija.
