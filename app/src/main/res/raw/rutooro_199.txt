NKUGONZA OKUKIRA BYONA
Original: Love Thee Better Lord
Composer: Ralph E. Hudson
1.
Nkugonza okukira byona, ebinsemeza amaiso,
Habw’obusinge obunyina, Obwikara omunda.
Chorus
Bingi, ebitutamanyire, Eby’engonzi zawe,
Biingi ebitutamanyire, (E)by’esagama yaawe.
2.
Nimanya onyikara haihi, okukira boona,
(O)buntekereza hali Iwe, nsemererwa muno.
3.
Okampa okusemererwa, n’omuhimbo omwingi,
Habw’engonzi zaawe enyingi, (o)bujune bugenda.
4.
Iwe Omujuni oungonza, ndigambaki owawe,
Hati obundaaba nindora omuhimbo mwingi?
