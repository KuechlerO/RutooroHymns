142
ENGONZI MBERE ZIRI
Original: There is Beauty all around
Composer: J. H. McNaughton
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 184
1.
Engonzi mbere ziri, tihaba naku,
Amaka gembabazi, gaijura engonzi;
Buli hantu businge, n’embazo z’emirembe,
Kusemererwa kwonka, habw’okugonza.
Chorus
Engonzi mu maka,
Kusemererwa kwonka, habw’okugonza.
2. 
(O)mu nju businge bwoka, habw’okugonza,
Busaho kibihiza, habw’okugonza;
Hona hona omu nju, kusemererwa kwonka,
(O)bwomeezi busemeza habw’okugonza.
3. 
Yesu asemererwa, ntugonzangana,
Okunoba n’ihali, tibisangwamu;
(A)maka gagonzangana, gaba agomuhendo,
Goleka oburungi, obwa Isiitwe.
4. 
Yesu nfora owewe, mbe n’okugonza,
Habw’okufa okwawe, (o)nfole muhyaka;
Iw(e) onkize omu bubi, mpumurre omuliiwe,
Mpa omugisa gwawe, Habw’okugonza.
