OMU NSI EY’OMU IGURU
Original: In the Land of Fadeless Day
Composer: Heart P. Danks
Luganda: 182
1.
Omu nsi ey’omu iguru, haliyo Orubuga;
Ruliikaraho bwona, busayo (o)mwirima.
Chorus
Ruhang(a) atuhumuza, Tihalibayo Okufa;
Omwirima tiguliyo, N’okubara emyaka.
2.   
Amalembo amarungi, ag’orubuga oru;
Engudo zoona zabbu, busayo (o)mwirima.
3.   
Haikara hakingwirwe, Omu rubuga oru;
(O)bwomeezi busemeza, busayo (o)mwirima.
4.   
Izooba tiryetaagwa, omu rubuga oru;
Yesu nuw(e) Omusana, busayo (o)mwirima.
