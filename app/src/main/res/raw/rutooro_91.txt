91
YESU AKATWETA HABUGENYI
Original: Called to the Feast
Composer: E. S. L Lorenz
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 172
1.   
Yes(u) akatweta ha bugenyi, Kuliira hamu n’abantu be,
Nukwo nu tulisangwa tuta, Yesu (O) bwaraija?
Chorus
Nukwo ab’oruganda, Yesu obwaraija,
Tuliba ha rubajuki, Yesu (o)bwaraija?
2.   
Ekondo eri ha mutwe gwe, N’ekitinis(a) ekiingi muno,
(O)kutiina kwingi kulibaho, (O)bwaragaruka.
3.   
Halibaho okumulikya, Ebitusereka abantu,
Nibiija okumanywa byona, (O)bwaragaruka.
4.   
Alisesererwa (a)banabe, (A)bajwekerwe ebirukwera;
Abasingwire habwa Yesu, (O)mujuni waabu.
5.   
Mukama tube embabazi, Amasiko gaitu gahweho,
Tukyanganuk(e) okukurora, (O)bworagaruka.
