203
NYINA EKIZINA EKINGONZA
Original: I Have A Song I Love to Sing
Luganda: 32
1.
Nyin(e) Ekizina ekingonza, kuruga nanfera;
Hal(i) Omujuni Mukama, Og(u) ayancungwire.
Chorus
Obu-yancungwire, okuruga nanjuna ndazinag(a) Ibaralye,
Og(u) yancugwire, ndasiimag(a) Ibara lya Yesu.
2.    
Kristo wang(e) amarra byona, kuruga nanfera;
Ngonza okukor(a) ebi asiima habw’okuncungura.
3.    
Nyin(a) obugumizo bwona, kuruga nanfera;
Kubinga(a) okutiina kwona, obuyancungwire.
4.    
Antegekiire ekiikaro, kuruga nanfera;
Ha ndiikar(a) ebiro byona, habw’okuncuugura.
