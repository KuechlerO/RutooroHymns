165
OKUSABA KWAHIKA
Original: Ti’s the Blessed Hour of Prayer
Composer: W. H. Doane
Luganda: 137
1.
Okusaba kwahika, tuteekan(e) emyoyo,
Twesorroze hali Yesu Omugonzibwa;
Tube n’okwikiriza, aratulindaga,
Buhumurro burungi, twegombe okwirra hali.
Chorus
Nungi esaaha egi, Nungi esaaha egi,
Buhumurro burungi, Twegomba okwirra hali.
2.   
Okusaba kwahika, Yesu obu airra haihi,
N’embabazi natweta hurr(a) irakalye;
Natugamba tutwale obujune bwaitu,
Buhumurro burungi, Twegomb(e) okwirra hali.
3.   
Okusaba kwahika, tutwale ebirengo,
Hali Yesu aratumarr(a) obujune,
Omu mitima yaitu aihamu obujune,
Buhumurro burungi twegomb(e) okwirra hali.
4.   
Okusaba kwahika, leka tumwesige,
Emigisa ye yoona aragituha;
Habw’okuba Mwesigwa, (a)ratugarukamu,
Buhumurro burungi, twegomb(e) okwirra hali.
