161
YESU OKATUNIHIZA ITWENA 
Original: Jesus Thou Hast Promissed
Composer:  W.O. Perkins
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 13
1.
Yesu okatunihiza itweena, Obu tuba hamu oba na itwe;
(E)kigambo hati nitukikiriza, Twikalemu hanu otusemeze.
Chorus
Yesu ija naitwe tukulinzire,
Tuhe (o)mugisa omu-kusorrokana.
2.   
(O)butoosa Mukama oba na itwe, Yongera oikale hamu na itwe;
Otaturugaho nitukwetaaga, Ija Ai Yesu tukuroleho.
3.   
Yesu tukonyere okukuramya, Ebizina byaitu bikusemeze;
Twikalege ntunihira (o)kugonza, Tuhe tweyongere kukusisana.
