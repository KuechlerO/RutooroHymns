10
NZINA OKUGONZA KWE  
Original: I will sing of Jesus’ Love
Composer: F. E. Belden
Key/Doh: Bb
1.
Nzina okugonza kwe, Ogu ayangondeze,
(A)karuga (O)mw’iguru rye, Okufa habwange.
Chorus
Nzina ha kugonza kwe,
Nuwe ndanyumizaaga,
Ayafiire habwange;
Nsiim(a) embabazi zaawe.
2.
Akabanza kungonza, Ntakamugondeze Uwe,
Nyowe ntakamwesire, akabanza kunserra.
3.
(O)kugonza kwawe Yesu, ka kuhamire muno,
Atanyina karungi, Kiki Iwe (o)kungonza.
4.
Yesu ayanyehaire, kiremire (o)kumwanga,
Okugonza kwa Yesu, nukumpambiriza.
