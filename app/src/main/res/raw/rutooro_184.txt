184
ISENYOWE NINKWETAAGA   
Original: Father, I Stretch My Hands
Composer:  S. Westy
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 143
1.
Isenyowe ninkwetaaga, Mucunguzi wange;
(O)kuganyira kwawe, Yesu otakunyihaho.
Chorus
NyikirizaYesu nuwe yafire ha bwange;
Esagama ekaseseka nyowe nganyirwe (e)bibi.
2.   
Niwe ogaba nyikirize, ndi mu maiso gaawe;
(E)kisembo kya Yesu Kristo, (o)kurugaho ninfa.
3.   
NyikirizeYesu Kristo, ompe (a)maani gawe;
(O)mu mbabazi zaawe Yesu, onkonyerege bwona.
4.   
Onkonyere otandeka, nkoku wanabiize;
Onyikazeho (o)mwoyo waawe, ndeke okubura.
5.   
Bundirora Yesu wange, ndisemererwa;
(E)mbabazize, Kigambo kye, nibyo bimpumuza.
