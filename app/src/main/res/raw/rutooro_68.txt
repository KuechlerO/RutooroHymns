68
MUGENDE OMU NSI ZOONA
Original: For My Sake and the Gospel Go
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 60
1.   
Muugende (o)mu nsi zoona mutebez(e) enjiri
Omu mahanga goona hali buli nuntu;
Nukwo betegereze ogu outuramya,
Kwali Ruhanga mali kandi okwajuna.
2.   
Mukama waitu Yesu otusobozese,
Okukoraga bwona (e)bitusemeriire;
Tweheyo Iwe hooli, (e)mibiri n’emyoyo,
Tuleteg(e) ebisembo n’okusemererwa.
3.   
Ai Ruhang(a) Isiitwe imukya (o)muli itwe,
Abagesi baingi bagende batule;
Beekambe hoona hoona kurarangy(a) enjiri,
Nibatebeza boona (a)bakuru n’abato.
