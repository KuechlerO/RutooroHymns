167
AKANFERA HA MUSALABA
Original: Down At the Cross
Composer: J. H. Stockton
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 19
1.
Akaanfera ha musalaba, nuho natungiir(e) obwomeezi;
Ninjunirw(a) Omu sagamaye, tumusiimega.
Chorus
Webale (O)mucunguzi, Osiimwege (o)butoosa;
Akanfera ha musalaba, tumusiimege.
2.
Mpunirra obuyancungwire, niiwe olem(a) obwomezi bwange;
(A)kanyereza ha musalaba, tumusiimege.
3.
(E)nsoro ey’omuhendo etwogya nsemererw(a) okwikara naiwe;
Okanjuna kandi olindiinda, tumusiimege.
4.
Ija hali Yesu ow’engonzi, hayo (o)mutiima gwawe gwona;
Nsab(a) okire omu sagamaye, tumusiimege.
