NKWETAAGA BUTOOSA
Original: I need Thee Every Hour
Composer: A. S. Hawks
Key/Doh: Ab
1.
Nkwetaaga butoosa, Iw(e) ow’embabazi;
Iraka erirungi, rireeta obusinge.
Chorus
Ego mali nkwetaaga, Nkwetaaga (o)butoosa;
Yes(u) ompe omugisa, Nyizire h’oli.
2.    
Nkwetaaga (o)butoosa, onyikale haihi;
Sitaani arairuka, ob(u) oba nanyowe.
3.    
Nkwetaaga (o)butoosa, (o)mu burungi n’obubi;
Ija omuli nyowe, ntunge Amaani gaawe.
4.    
Nkwetaaga (o)butoosa; (o)kunkoz(a) ebi ogonza;
Emirago yaawe, ogihikiirize.
5.    
Nkwetaaga (o)butosa; Iwe Arukwera;
Nfoora owaawe mali, lw(e) ow’embabazi.
