19
IKARA NA NYOWE
Original: Abide with Me
Composer: William H. Monk
Key/Doh: Eb
1.   
Ikara ma nyowe (e)kiro kinu, Omu mwirima ogukwasire;
Tinyina omukonyezi weena, Akonyer(a) abanaku nkonyera.
2.   
Buire bw’obwomeezi buira, Buky(a) ahonaaho buba rwebagyo;
Ebyensi byona biba nk’esaabu, Iwe atahinduk(a) otansiga.
3.   
Ninkwetaaga musana n’ekiro, Niiwe wenk(a) onsinguza Setani;
Ondi nooh(a) asobor(a) okumgumya, (O)mu mbeho n’ekyanda otansiga.
4.   
(A)banyanzigwa tintiina oroho, Onsinguzeege ebibi byona;
Onsemezege (o)mu kutuntura, (O)kufa n’ekituuro bitiinisa.
5.   
(O)bundiba ndi haihi n’okufa, Ofooke taara omu mwirima;
Ontekerezese (e)kondo yange, Hati n’omu kuf(a) otalinsiga.
