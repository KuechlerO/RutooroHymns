92
ENGWARA YA YESU ERIGAMBA
Original: When the Trumpet of the Lord
Composer: J. M. Black
Luganda: 168
1.   
Engwara ya Yesu erigaamba naija ha bicu,
Ha kiro eki ekikuru muno;
Abarukwer(a) abali (o)mu nsi balisorozebwa,
Obubalyet(a) amabara ndibaho.
Chorus
(O)bubaliba nibatweta, (O)bubaliiba nibatwete;
(O)bubaliba nibatweta, Ha kiro eki nanyowe ndibaho.
2.   
Tulirora abafu mu Kristo nibahumbuka,
Banyina (e)kitinisa kya Ruhanga;
Nibeet (a) abakomerwe (o)kugenda omu Iguru,
Obubalyet(a) amabara ndibaho.
3.   
Ab’oruganda leka itwe tukole n’amaani,
Katutebeze abantu ha Yesu;
(O)mu kasumi katali kaingi aligaruka,
Obubalyeet(a) amabara ndibaho.
