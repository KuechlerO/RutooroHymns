210
OKUSEMERERWA KWINGI
Original: Joys are Flowing like a River
1.
Okusemererwa kwingi habw’okwija (o)kw’Omwoyo,
Okwikar(a) ebiro byona omuli abamwesiga.
Chorus
Obusinge bwa Ruhanga,
Nibungumy(a) omutima,
(O)mu bigonzi Yesu agamba,
Culer(a) iwe, humura.
2.   
Obusinge n’omuhimbo biija n’omuhumuza,
Tweyonger(e) okwikiriza, kandi tumworobere.
3.   
Nk’enjur(a) egwa ha nsi yaitu, nk’omusa(a) ogw’izooba,
N’Omwoyo tumutunga (o)mu mulingo nugwo gunu.
4.   
(A)bantu baingi beeyongire, kuba (a)baana ba Yesu,
Nomusana guli hoona omu irungu elyomere.
5.   
Kakukuru okujunwa okuruga hal’Iwe,
Tuliikar(a) omu businge tuhumule na Yesu.
