60
TUKUGIZA MUKAMAWAITU
Original: Our Sabbath Worship  
1.   
Tumukugiza Mukama waitu, Ha kiro kye ekya Sabbiti;
Ha kiro kya hati turamya Yesu, Niiwe watulinzire bwona.
2.   
Omu kitabu ekirukwera, Ak’okurorraho Sabiiti;
Hagati yaawe n’abantu baawe, Niiwe watulinzire bwona.
3.   
Omu Bunabbi twetegeriize, Ekiro kinu ekirindwa;
Abakuhurra basemererwa, Abakugonza nubo baawe.
4.   
Abanabbi n’Abakwenda boona, Bakwataga Sabbiti yaawe;
Bahurrag(a) Iwe Mukama waabu, (A)yaragiire ngu bahumule.
5.   
Ab’oruganda bagenzi baitu, Tukole Yesu ebyagonza;
Tusemererweg(e) itwena hamu, Ajuna boon(a) abamuhurra.
