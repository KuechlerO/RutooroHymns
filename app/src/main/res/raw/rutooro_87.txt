OMUSANA GWAKA HANSOZI
Original: There is Sunlight on the Hilltop
Composer: M.T. Haughey
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 45
1.    
(O)musana gwaka ha nsozi, Omusan(a) omurungi;
(O)mu misiri na ha Nyanja, Omusana Guliyo.
Baitu omusana mwingi gwaka (o)mu Mwoyo gwange;
Yesu (o)kuba omuli nyowe, ampa omusana gwe.
Chorus
Omusana omurungi, Gwaka (o)mu mwoyo gwange,
Yesu Iwe onsemeza, Niiwe (o)musana gwange.
2.   
Nk’orugoy(e) orukuzire, Njurama obujune;
Njeweka okusemererwa, N’okuranganwa kwawe.
(O)mu maka (a)g’ekitinisa, (A)gawantekaniriize;
Okagahunda kurungi, Omu kugonza kwawe.
3.   
Okancungura Mujuni, (o)mutuma ngukuhaire;
Omusana Iw(e) ogwompa, Gwakege buli kiro.
(O)mu maka (a)g’ekitinisa, (A)gawantekaniriize;
Ndigataha nsemeriirwe, Omu mutuma gwange.
