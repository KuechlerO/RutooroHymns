110
MWANDARA GWA RUHANGA 
Original: Rock of Ages Cleft for Me
Composer: T. Hastings
Key/Doh: C
Luganda: 193
1.   
Mwandara gwa Ruhanga, Ogwayasirwe ira
Ha bwange omusiisi, Omul(i) Iwe onsereka;
Hal(i) amaani g’ekibi, Ondinde onsagike.
2.   
Emirimo y’engaro, N’okwekamba kw’omwoyo
(O)bujune n’okucura, Byona tibisobora;
Okunyihah(o) ebibi, Niiwe wenka (O)mujuni.
3.   
Tinyina ekimpayo, Ndi omunaku mali,
Nyesiga Iwe wenka, (A)yanferiire ha muti;
Ndi omubi onyogye, Onganyire (I)we ntafa.
4.   
(O)bundihika (o)mw’iguru, Ndesir(e) ebiba byange,
(O)bundirora Iwe Yesu, Ndisemererwa muno;
Ndiba ntakyasobora, Kwenyumizaaga nyenka.
