201
ABAGESI BARAHA?  
Original: Oh where are the Reapers?
Composer: Eben E. Rexford
Key/Doh: G
1.
Abagesi baraha okuleeta abesirwe, (o)kuruga omu biibi?
Abeekambi omu kukora (o)kutajwaha buli guhoire.
Chorus
Inywe abagesi n’oha aija kukwataniza omulimo gwa Yesu;
Arakonyera (o)kubaleeta, okubaih(a), omu bibi by’ensi?
2.   
Mugende omu nguudo mubaserre, dora baliyo mugende mubaleete;
Muserre muno buli muntu, mubaleete babe abaana be.
3.   
Emisiri yoona dora okweri abantu balinzire, okubeeta abakozi bake;
Omulimo mwingi, Tubaleete obutalindirra.
4.   
Mwije bwangu abantu ba Mukama tusorooze, Mukama abagonza;
Gumisiriza dora naija, (o)rasemererwa habw’okwekamba.
