147
KANGENDEGE NAIWE YESU
Original: O Let Me Walk with Thee
Composer: Edwin Barnet
Luganda: 14
1.
Kangendege naiwe Yesu, Nk’Enoka yakuserraga,
Iwe nkwata ha mukono, Kandi obaze nanyowe,
Omuhanda gw’omwirima, Kangendege naiwe Yesu.
2.
Kugenda nyenka nindemwa, Ekiyaga kingi muno,
Emitego ya Sitani, Hamu n’amahege goona,
Ninkusab(a) obimaleho, Kangendege naiwe Yesu.
3.
Obunyikara na Iwe, Eby’ensi byoona bintama,
Kangendege n’obumanzi, Nkwate Ebendera yawe,
Olimpikya mu Sayuni, Nindubata hamu naiwe.
