158
RUHANGA ONYIRIZE
Original: Nearer My God To Thee
Composer: L. Mason
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 152
1.
Ruhanga onyirize, haihi na’Iwe,
N’obundiba kubi, mu bujune;
Okukwirra haihi, nyeyongerege,
Okukwirra haihi, haihi na’Iwe.
2.
Bwire bw’obwomezi, nibuira,
Tiharoho nomu arankonyera;
Ninkwesengereza, onyirize haihi,
Onyirize haihi, hali na’Iwe.
3.
Okumanyisibwa, kuruga hooli,
Ahaswekerirwe, nukwo hazooke;
N’abamalaika banyoleke hooli,
Banyoleke hooli, Haihi na’Iwe.
4.
Ninsemerererwa, (e)mbabazi zawe,
Habw’okuganyira omwiru waawe;
Ruhang(a) otandeka mu bitahikire,
Mu bitahikire, hara na’Iwe.
5.
Ira nditahayo, mwa Isenyowe,
Obundimalirra ngendo zange;
Ekirinsemeza, Yesu (o)kunyiriza,
Yesu (o)kunyiriza, haihi na’Iwe.
