32
MU RUGENDO RWANGE RWONA
Original: All the way My Savior Leads Me
Composer: R. Lowry
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 155
1.   
Mu rugendo rwange rwona, Yesu anyebembere;
Ndasobora nta kuhyema, Andabize omu biingi?
(O)mu kubonabona kwange, Nuwe ndayesigaga,
Nuwe Omuliisa wange, Angabir(a) obwomeezi,
Nuwe Omuliisa wange, Angabir(a) obwomeezi.
2.   
Mu rugendo rwange rwona, Yesu anyebembera;
(O)bundizirwa (a)banyanzigwa, Nuw(e) alibasingura.
Kandi nuwe alimpikya, Owe omu businge,
N’obundirumwa enjara, Nuwe alinyigusa,
N’obundirumwa enjara, Nuwe alinyigusa.
3.  
Mu rugendo rwange rwona, Yesu anyebembera;
Obumba nyin(a) okujwaha, Nuwe mpumurro yange,
Kandi obumba n’iroho, mimiro ekarakaire,
Nuwe Mwandara haruga, Amaizi G’obwomeezi,
Nuwe Mwandara haruga, Amaizi G’obwomeezi.
4.   
Mu rugendo rwange rwona, Yesu anyehembera;
Iwe Omujuni wange, ayankiriz(e) okufa,
Ndakukuzaaga bwona, Ebiro byange byona,
Ndazinaga n’omuhimbo, Ninkunyumiza Yesu,
Ndazinaga n’omuhinibo, Ninkunyumiza Yesu.
