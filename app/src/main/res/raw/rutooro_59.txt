59
EKIRO KY’OKUSEMERERWA
Original: O Day of Rest and Gladness
Composer: L. Mason
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 82
1.   
(E)kiro ky’okusemererwa, kandi n’obusinge,
Kimarah(o) obujune, (e)kisemiire muno,
Kir(o) eki (a)bantu boona, abagonza Yesu,
Besooroleza hamu, Okumukugiza.
2.   
(E)kiro ekyasoroirwe, (O)mu maiso gaYesu,
Nk’omusiri (o)murungi, Oguitirrw(e) amaizi,
(A)garuhura iroho, Hal(i) akarakaire,
Nk’orusozi Pisiga, Turora Kanaani.
3.   
(E)kiro ky’ekihumuro, Hatujaguliza,
Okugonza kwa Yesu, tukutekereza;
Tutunga amaani ge, Gatukoza byona,
Tusaba atuhikye, (O)mu maka ge gali.
