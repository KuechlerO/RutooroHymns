24
OBUTUIKARA HAMU NA YESU
Original: When We Walk with the Lord
Composer: Daniel B. T.
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 12
1.   
Obutuikara ahamu na Yesu, Atuleet(a) omu irisizo lye;
Tukora (e)byagonza aikara naitwe, Kandi naabo abamwesiga.
Chorus
Tumwesige, Busah(o) ondi n’omu,
Rundi Yesu waitu nuwe twesigege.
2.   
Busaho mwirima, busaho n’ebicu, Yesu nuwe Musana gw’ensi;
Titutiinisibwa, busaho kwiruka, Aikara naitwe bwona.
3.   
Tukora ntwegonza, titutiinisibwa, Yesu alituuha empeera;
Leka tumwesige, katumworobere, Emigisa ye tugihaabwe.
4.   
Titukamanyire (o)kugonza kwe kwona, Okuhiky(a) obutulyehayo;
Naatuha (o)kwerinda, kandi n’okwegonza, Hamu n’abo (a)bamworobera.
