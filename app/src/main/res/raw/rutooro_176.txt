176
AHULIIRE WEENA
Original: Whosoever Heareth
Composer: Philip P. Bliss
Key/Doh: D
Luganda: 109
1.
Ahuliire weena atebeze! Enjiri ya Yesu omu nsi yoona;
Serra buli muntu omugambire: Ija haluwe Yesu.
Chorus
Ija haluwe, Ija haluwe, Kirangirr(e) omu nsozi n’omu byeya;
Ngu Yesu nayeta ayabuzire, Ija haluwe wenka.
2.   
Ija bwang(u) ogaruke haluwe; Orwigi rw’embabazi rukyaroho;
Nuwe Omuhanda N’amananu, Ija haluwe Yesu.
3.   
Amugonza weena amunihiza, (O)kumugumya omu nsi Ey’ebizibu
Yesu nuwe bwomeez(i) obutahwaho, Ija haluwe wenka.
