15
ENU SAAHA Y’OKUSABA  
Original: Sweet Hour of Prayer
Composer: W. B. Bradbury
Key/Doh: D
Luganda: 135
1.   
Enu saaha y’Okusaba, Ruga ha bigambo by’ensi;
Tugende hali Isiitwe, Tumusab(e) atukonyere;
Obutuba tujwahire, Tuhabwe okuhumura;
Atujun(e) omunyanzigwa, Omu saaha yokusaba.
2.   
Omu saaha y’okusaba, Mutwarr(a) ebigambo byange;
Hali ogu Omwesigwa, Asemez(a) abamwetaaga;
Abamwebing(a) abairiza, (O)bubesig(a) ekigambo kye;
Kamwoleke buli kintu, Omu saaha y’okusaba.
3.   
Omu saaha y’okusaba, Onkwatirre (o)mu kwohebwa;
Onkyanganuky(e) omutuma, Onyolek(e) amaka gaitu;
(O)bwolinjweka obutafa, Ompair(e) ekisembo kyawe;
Mpiriirwe ndiziina muno, Mpikire oku owaitu.
