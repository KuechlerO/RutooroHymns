113
OKUHIKYA OBWALIIJA
Original: Till He come! O Let the Words
Composer: R.Cooil
1.   
Okuhikya (o)bwaliija, Tulye obugenyi bwe
Baitu nubutwoleka okufa n’okwija kwe;
Tuleke (o)kutuntura Tali hara naija.
2.   
Abagenzi baitu obubaturugaho, Naitwe tusigaraho,
Amasu gatukwata; Nubwo twijuka bwango
Yes(u) alibahumbura.
3.   
Nukwo turarwanaga okurwan(a) okurungi,
Turaasobora tuta, Okubinga Setani?
Omwoyo Arukwera nuw(e) alitusoroza.
4.   
Kandi Yesu (o)bwaliija, alisoroza boona,
Abakuru n’abato, abamuikiriza,
Alibateraniza, Boon(a) omu bukamabwe.
