EMISIRI EYEZIRE
Original: Far and Near the Fields are Teeming
Composer: J.B.O. Clemm
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 94
1.     
Emisiri eyezire eby’okugesa biingi,
Biri omu biikaro byona mu bihanga mu byeya.
Chorus
Ai Mukama wa byona otuhurrege Yesu,
Tum(a) abantu baawe boona omu mulimo gwawe.
2.      
Otumege nyenkya kara kandi n’omu ihangwe,
Yongeraho n’orwebagyo banoge hoona hoona.
3.      
Mukama akatutuma (o)kusoroz(a) amagesa,
Okuhikya obwaliija alituha empeera.
