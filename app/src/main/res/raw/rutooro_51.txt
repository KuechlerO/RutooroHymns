51
YESU ALI HA RWIGI
Original: There’s a Stranger at the Door
Composer: E. O. Excell
Key/Doh: Db
Luganda: 102
1.      
Yesu ali ha rwigi, Ali aho; Akutegeriiz(e) iwe, Kingura;
Omutaahy(e) atagenda, Arukwera Mukama;
Omwana wa Ruhanga , Ali aho.
2.   
Dora hurra naakweta, Mujuni; Leker(a) ensi oije, (o)rahuka;
Ali aho ha rwigi, (A)kuletir(e) obusinge;
Makune g’ibara lye, Mujuni.
3.
Kingura omutuma, Ataahe; Obworakerererwa, Nagenda;
Nganjani yaawe mali, Ayoroby(a) omutuma;
Ikiriz(a) okingule, Mutaahye.
4.
Akarug(a) omw’iguru, habwawe; (A)ketwek(a) omusalaba, Ojunwe;
Oteburanganiza, (I)kiriz(a)okugonza kwe;
Nayeranga Mujuni, Kingura.
