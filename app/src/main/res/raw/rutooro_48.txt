48
SEMERERWA HABW’EKI 
Original: We Have Heard
Composer: W. J. Kirkpatrick
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 108
1. 
Semererwa habw’eki, Yesu acungura;
Katukitwale hoona, Yesu acungura;
Manyis(a) omu nsi zoona, Ha nsozi n’amazinga;
Ruhanga natugamba, Yesu acungura.
2.
Tebez(a) abantu boona, Yesu acungura;
Manyisa abasiisi, Yesu acungura;
Amazinga gazine, N’eby’omu maizi byona;
Ensi etoke muno, Yesu acungura.
3.
Muzin(e) inywe (a) barwani, Yesu acungura;
Habw’okufa kwe kwona, Yesu acungura;
N’obwob(a) omu bujune, Hamu n’okutuntura;
N’omu kufa zina ngu, Yesu acungura.
4.
Imukya iraka lyawe, Yesu acungura;
Ensi zoona zigambe, Yesu acungura
Obwomeezi bwa busa, Tebez(a) omu mahanga;
Okusingura kwaitu, Yesu acungura.
