69
NOOHA ALIRWANA N’OMUNYANZIGWA
Original: Who is on the Lord’s Side?
1.   
Nooha alirwana n’omunyanzigwa?
Nooha aligenda okutebeza? Nooha aliguma okusalirwa?
N’obujune bwona habwa Mukama?
Chorus
Tuli bairu booha, Tuli baYesu;
Twali ba Setani, twamufuruka.
2.   
Titirukuserra ekitinisa, Eky’ebiro binu baitu (e)kyaYesu;
Baitu okugonza kwa Yesu Kristo,
Nikuhambiriza abacungurwa.
3.   
Yesu (a)katugura n’esagamaye,
Kandi akanywana hamu naitwe; Tuligaya tuta embabazi ze?
Katusige byona habw’ibara lye.
4.   
Okurwana kwingi n’obukuroho,
Omujuni Yesu alisingura; Titurukutiina abanyanzigwa,
Obu tuliguma baliiruka.
