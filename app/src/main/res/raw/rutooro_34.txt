34
ONKONYEREGE NYESIGE
Original: My Hope is Built on Nothing Less
Composer: W. Batchelder Bradbury
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 69
1.   
Onkonyerege Nyesige, (E)sagama y’Omucunguzi;
Ndeke (o)kwesiga eby’ensi, Baitu ngumire hal(i) lwe
Chorus
Kangumire hali Yesu, Byon(a) eby’ensi butarumu;
Byon(a) eby’ensi butarumu.
2.   
Byon(a) eby’ensi n’obubiija, Kantekerez(e) ebya Yesu;
(O)mu bujune n’omu ndwara, Tolinsiga Mucunguzi.
3.   
(A)mananu gawe gangumya, Na byon(a) ebiwanihize;
N’obunferwa (e)byangc byona, Kankwesige Iwe wenka.
4.   
Akasumi kali haihi, Kanyeheyo ansemeze;
Anyogye (o)mu sagama ye, Ampe ak’okurorraho.
5.   
Empero obwerihika, Ensange nyetekaniize;
Nyina akokurorraho, Ak’Omwoyo Arukwera.
