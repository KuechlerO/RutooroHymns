BOONA ABAGWIJAGIRA
Original: Them also which Sleep
1.   
Boona abagwijagira Nibesiga Yesu,
Tibaligaruk(a) okufa Okwâ€™okusalirwa.
2.   
Itwena abali hanu Ha kituuro kinu
Tusendekeriize omu, outwagondeze.
3.   
Tuleke okuganyaga, Tumwesige Yesu
Ayatunihiize itwe abamuikiriize.
4.   
Itwena ebiro binu Twetekanirizege,
Omu ngeso zaitu zoona Twikale (o)muliiwe.
