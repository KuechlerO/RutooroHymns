105
OMU RUGENDO TAHO OMUTUMA
Original: Look for the Waymarks
Composer: Franklin Edson Belden
1.   
Omu rugendo tah(o) omutuma, Obunabbi bw’obukama bbuna;
Obukama obu bukahwaho, Hati omugenzi, oli nkaha?
Chorus
Wetegereze obw’okurorrah(o) obu,
Obukama obu bukahingura;
Cumitiriza ebigambo bya Nabbi, Emper(o) ehikire.
2.   
Ab(a) Asura bakalema hoona, Hanyuma ha kaimuk(a) Abamedi;
Nubw(o) Abayonani, n’Abarumi, Hati tuli omu kasumi ki?
3.   
Obukama (o)bw’ekyoma n’ibumba, Nabwo buli haihi okuhwaho!
Hanyuma kiki (e)kirahondera? Kristo naija n’ekitinisa!
