218
BULI KIGAMBO EKIBI 
Original: Angry Words, O Let Them Never
Composer: Dr. H.R. Palmer
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 150
1.
Buli Kigambo ekibi, Inywe mukyerindege,
Lindag(a) Omutima gwawe, hamu n’orulimi rwawe.
Chorus
Mugonzanganege, Yesu nukw(o) agamba
(Nabaana be, Nkabaana be)
Mworobere ekiragirokye, Katugonzege, nabatunobere,
(Nkabaana be, Nkabaana be)
Twikalege Abaana be.
2. 
Okugonz(a) Okwamazima kuleeta amagezi,
Enobi eri ngeso ya Sitani (o)munyanzigwa.
3. 
Ebigambo byekiniga, bireterez(a) ebibi,
Bisumurra enjegere, z’obusinge bwamaka.
