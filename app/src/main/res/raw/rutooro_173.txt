173
NINGIRORA NENANATA
Original: The Cleansing Wave
Composer: Mrs. Phoebe Palmer
Key/Doh: Eb
1.
Ningirora nenanata ensoro ey’amaani,
Yesu Mukama najuna, dor(a) orubajurwe.
Chorus
Esagama ye enyogya, Nesobor(a) okunsemeza,
Webale Yesu okunyogya! Kaneyogya nesemeza.
2.   
Obwomeezi obuhyaka, habw’esagamaye,
Ebingamu ekibi kyona, eiyasesire.
3.  
Omusanagwe ogw’iguru ogunyebembera,
N’omutima ogusemiire, entebe yaYesu.
4.   
Iguru lizire ha nsi habw’esagamaye,
Iwe Yesu nokimanya Iw(e) ayanferiire.
