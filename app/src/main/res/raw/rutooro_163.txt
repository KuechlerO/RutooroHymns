163
CHULERA, CHULERA, MUKAMA NAIJA
Original: Tread Softly
Composer: William Howard D.
Key/Doh: Bb
Luganda: 146
1.
Chulera, chulera, Mukama naija;
Chulera ohulirize Mukama.
Chorus
Rubata mpora, mu maiso ga Yesu,
Rubata mpora oli h’arukwera.
2.   
Chulera chulera omu Yekaru,
Huliriza (o)bukwenda bw’embabazi.
3.   
Tweculez(e) itwena nitumusaba,
Tusobole kumanya (e)birungi bye.
4.   
Chulera akuhe h’ambabazi ze,
Chulera olindirrege Mukama.
