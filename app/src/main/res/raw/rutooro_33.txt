33
IWE YAHWE ARUKWERA
Original: Guide me, O Great Jehovah
Composer: W. Williams
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 156
1.   
Iwe YahweArukwera, Nyebembera Mukama,
Mu bugara bwange bwona, Onyikalege haihi,
Onkwatirre, Ongumize,
(O)mu mukono gw’amaan, (O)mu mukono gw’amaani.
2.   
Iwe (E)nsoro z’obwomeezi Hali abaana baawe,
Itirra emyoyo yaitu, Obingemu ekibi,
 Iw(e) enyomyo ey’omurro,
 Oli ngabu yaitu, Oli ngabu yaitu.
3.   
(O)butulihika ha musa Gw’ekyambu Yorudani,
(O)kutiina kwange kuhweho, Ompikye (O)mu Kanani,
Nyeyongerege butoosa,
Okukukugizaaga, Okukukugizaaga.
