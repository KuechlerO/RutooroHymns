43
MUKAMA NINGONZA OKUBA OMURUNGI 
Original: Lord Jesus, I Long to be Parfectly Whole
Composer: W. G. Fischer
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 114
1.    
Mukama ningonz(a) okub(a) omurungi,
Oikaleg(e) omu mutuma gwange;
(O)marremu kimu ebitasemlire, Onyeze okukira ebirika.
Chorus
Onyogye (o)mu sagama yaawe,
(E)by’okusiisa byange byona bihweho.
2.   
Ndolerra noyema omu Iguru, Onkonyere (o) kuruga omu kibi;
Nyehaireyo (o)mu mikono yaawe,
Nyogya n’esagama nyerere titiiti.
3.   
Yesu kinu nikyo ndukukusaba,
Nk’oku wafire habw’ebibi byange;
Onyogeze kimu ebibi byange, Onyeze okukira ebirika.
4.   
Norora nk’okundikukunihira,
Ija ntung(e) omutuma omurungi;
Iwe atabing(a) abanyakwija, Onyogeze kimu omu sagama.
