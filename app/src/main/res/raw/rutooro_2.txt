2
IWE ARUKWERA
Original: Holy, Holy, Holy
Composer: John Bacchus Dykes
Key/Doh: E
Luganda: 1
1.
Iwe Arukwera, Mukama wa byona.
Ntukuhaisaniza hamu n’okuzina;
Iwe Arukwera, Ow’amaani goona,
Ruhanga omu ow’obusinge.
2.
Iwe Arukwera , Nibakukugiza,
Na bamaraika nibakuzinira;
Kerubi, Serafi, Bakuramya boona,
Ruhanga omu ow’obwomeezi.
3.     
Iwe Arukwera, Mukama Ruhanga,
Ebyomeezi byona nibikukugiza.
Iwe Arukwera, Ow’amaani goona,
Ruhanga omu ow’obusinge.
