81
NINYEHAYO YESU HOOLI
Original: All To Jesus I Surrender
Composer: W. Scott Weeden
Luganda: 122
1. 
Ninyehayo Yesu hooli omubiri n’omwoyo;
Ninkugonza ninkwesiga nyikaleg(e) Iwe hooli.
Chorus
Ninyehayo Hooli, Nyizire, hooli;
Nyiriza MukamaYesu, Nyizire hooli.
2. 
Ninyehayo hooli Yesu ndi omu maiso gaawe;
Nyihaho eby’ensi byona onfoole (o)mwana wawe.
3. 
Ninyehayo Yesu hooli nyogya nyere tititi;
Mpa Omwoyo Arukwera nyikalege ndi waawe.
4. 
Ninyehayo Yesu hooli mpuliir(e) onyikirize;
N’omuhimbo gw’okujunwa ninkusiima Mukama.
