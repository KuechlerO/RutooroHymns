58
IJUKA EKIRO YESU EKIYATUHAIRE
Original: Don’t Forget the Sabbath
Composer: W. B. Bradbury
Key/Doh: C
Luganda: 84
1.   
Ijuka ekiro Yesu kiyatuhaire, Ekirukukir(a) ebindi oburungi;
Kinyin(a) okuhumur(a) kuleet(a) omugisa,
Oguruga omu iguru hali Mukama.
Chorus
Tutangirra, Tutangirra ekiro kya Yesu;
Tutangirra, Tutangirra Sabiiti (e)nungi.
2.   
Aha kiro kinu nuho tumuramiza,
Ninyowe Muhanda hamu n’obwomeezi;
Obutumuhondera Mucunguzi waitu,
Nuw(e) alitunywis(a) amaizi g’obwomeezi.
3.   
Aha kiro kinu nubwo tumukugiza,
(O)kuzinira Yesu nganjani y’abaato;
Hali abo (a)bagwire oli w’embabazi,
Itwe tukusiima Omujuni waitu.
