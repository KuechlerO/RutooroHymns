TWARA ENSI OMPE YESU
Original: Take the World but Give Me Jesus
Composer: J. R. Sweney
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 145
1.   
Twara ensi ompeYesu, (E)byâ€™ensi tibikaraho;
Okugonza kwawe Yesu, kuroho (e)biro byona.
Chorus
(E)mbabazi zaYesu nyingi, Ansemez(a) omutuma,
Nuwe ayatucungwire, Natuha obwomeezi.
2.  
Twara ensi ompe Yesu, Nuw(e) ansemeza Omwoyo;
Obwaba (o)mulinzi wang, Busaho (e)kyokutiina.
3.   
Twara ensi ompe Yesu, Bwona amulikire;
(O)mu rugendo rwange rwona, Habemu omusana.
4.   
Twara ensi ompe Yesu, Ninyesig(a) okufa kwe;
Okuhikya obwaliija, (O)bwaliizira ha bicu.
