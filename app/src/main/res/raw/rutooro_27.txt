27
IBARALYA YESU LISEMIIRE
Original: How Sweet the Name of Jesus Sounds
Composer: A. R. Reinagle
Luganda: 24
1.   
Ibara eri (e)rya Yesu lisemiire muno,
Hali abaikiriza, Abaanabe mali.
2.   
Lihumuza omutuma, Liiham(u) okutiina,
Liculeeza obujune, Lisemez(a) emputa.
3.   
Ibara eringondeze, Lifook(e) omwandara,
Hanyombekere (e)nju yaange, Nuho nyesereka.
4.   
Yesu Omuliisa wange, Enganjani yange,
Ninkusiima Omukama, n’obwomeezi bwange.
5.   
Hati ndatebezaaga, E’ibara lyawe,
Obundihika ha kufa, niryo limpumuze.
