TEERA ENGOMA MANYISA HOONA
Original: Lift up the Trumpet
Composer: George E. Lee
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 161
1.   
Teera engoma manyisa hoona, Yesu aliija bwangu;
Musemererwe inyw(e) abagenzi, Yesu aliija bwangu.
Chorus
Yesu naija, Yesu naija, Yesu aliija bwangu.
2.   
Twara kinu (o)mu mahanga goona, Yesu aliija bwangu;
Naija n’ekitinisa ekingi, Yesu aliija bwangu.
3.   
Imukya amais(o) omu nsi zoona, Yesu aliija bwangu;
(O)bulemu n’ekiniga (o)mu bantu, Yesu aliija bwangu.
4.   
(A)magezi geyongir(e) omu bantu, Yesu aliija bwangu;
Enjara n’emisisa omu nsi, Yesu aliija bwangu.
5.   
Enjiri ehike bwangu (o)mu nsi, Yesu aliija bwangu;
Byon(a) ebi bitwoleka itwe ngu, Yesu aliija bwangu.
