TUKYANGANUKA YESU NAIJA 
Original: Joy to the World 
Composer: G. F. Handel
Key/Doh: D
Luganda: 166
1.
Tukyanganuke Yesu naija! Ens(i) emutangirre,
Bul(i) omu ayetekanize, Nebihangi byona,
(O)mw’iguru n’omu nsi; Byona nibimukugiza.
2.    
Yesu naija okulema! Itwe tukugize,
Ensozi n’ebyeya byona, Nibisemererwa
Nibimukugiza, Byona nibimukugiza.
3.
Yesu naija kusemeza, (E)bintu byon(a) eby’ensi,
(A)mahwa gona ag’ekibi, tigalimerayo okusisa ensi,
Okusisa ensi, (O)mukyeno guliba guhoire.
4.    
Naija kulet(a) obusinge!
Mu mahanga gona; Galiror(a) ekitinisa,
Galisemererwa galimukugiza habw’okugonzakwe,
Habw’okugonza kwe okwingi.
