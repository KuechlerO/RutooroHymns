134
MUTAKORA OBUBI
Original: Cease to Do Evil 
1.
Mutakor(a) obubi, Mutalikungana;
Muli ba Ruhanga, Baana baYesu.
2.
Kristo muculeezi, tanyina kamogo;
N’abanabe abato, muculerege.
3.
Haliyo omubi, Nabeserrekerra;
Naserra okubohya, N’okubaita.
4.
Mutalimuhurru, N’obumulemerwa;
Okumurwanisaga, Mwange ekibi.
5.
Yesu Kristo nuwe, Omukama wanyu;
N’abaana be abato, Mumworobere.
