NINYETAAGA OKUHURRA
Original: Sing Them Over Again Philip Bliss
1.  
Ninyetaaga okuhurra Haby’okucungurwa,
Nyeyonger(e) okubimanya, (E)bigambo bya Yesu;
Nibyo bigambo kwo, Eby’okucungurwa,
Chorus
(E)bigambo birungi muno eby’okucungurwa,
(E)bigambo birungi muno Eby’okucungurwa.
2.   
Mukama nuwe atuha Eby’okusemererwa,
Ebireeta omusiisi hali obwomeezi;
Mukama (e)byatuha bituhikya haali,
3.   
Ebigambo bya Mukama nubwo obwomeezi,
Bituuha obusinguzi n’amaani g’omwoyo;
Ebibi bihwaho omu ibara lyawe.
