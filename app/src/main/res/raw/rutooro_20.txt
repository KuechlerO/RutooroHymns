NYAMUSANA N’EKIRO   
Original: Day is Dying in the West
Composer: W. F. Sherwin
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 92
1.   
Nyamusana n’ekiro, Iguru limanyisa,
(O)kutegereza Yesu, aligaruka bwango, Ai Yesu.
Chorus
Iwe Arukwera, ai Yesu; Omuhangi w’iguru,
Omuhangi w’ensi enu, Ai Yesu.
2.   
Mukama w’obwomeezi, Otukonyere (o)mu nsi,
Kanyis(a) amaani gaawe, Hali abakugonza, Ai Yesu.
3.   
Ens(i) enu y’obujune, Itwe otukwatirre,
(O)tulinde n’embabazi, Otuhe obwomeezi, Ai Yesu.
4.   
(O)butulihaabw(a) ekondo, Ha kiro ekikuru,
Bamaraika boona, Tuliba n’omuhimbo, Ai Yesu.
