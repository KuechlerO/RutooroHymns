194
YESU ALIREMA
Original: Jesus shall Reign
Composer: John Hatton
Luganda: 15
1.
Yesu alirema hoona, hoona(a) omusana ha gwaka,
Obukama bwe bulirema kuhikya ebiro byoona.
2. 
Abantu (a)b’endimi zoona, bakugizeg(e) Ibara lye,
Abaana be barangirrege, oburungi bw’ibaralye.
3. 
Kuganyira kwe kw’amahano, (a)basibe basumururwe
(A)bajwahire barahumuzibwaga, ab’enjara nibaiguta
4. 
Buli muntu asemererwe, akugize Ruhanga waitu,
Bamalaika nibaziina, ensi egambe, Amiina.
