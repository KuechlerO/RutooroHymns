156
ABASIISI NABETA INYWENA
Original: Seeking The Lost
Composer: W. A. Ogden.
Luganda: 228
1.
Abasiisi nabeta inywena, abahabire ha busozi;
Yesu nabeta mwije inywena, Yesu nabeta kiro kinu.
Chorus
Abahabiire hona hona, let(a) abahabire boona boona;
Omw’igana ery’Omucunguzi, Ayafire ha bwaitu itweena.
2.   
Leta boona oboleke Yesu, Abendwaire ezomu mwoyo;
Balete hali Omucunguzi, mu bwomezi obutahwaho.
3.   
Kangende hali Yesu nanyeta, Nyikale nawe buli kiro;
Nkiz(e) abo abali mu kufa, mbolek(e) omuhanda gwa Yesu.
