112
OBUNYIKARA HA BIGERE
Original: At the Feet of Jesus
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 46
1.   
(O)bunyikarra ha bigere bya Yesu ansemeza,
Ngonza okwikara nawe, Okuhurr(a) ebyagamba,
(O)bunyikara haihi nawe, Ncumitiriza Uwe
Okugonza kwe okwingi, Okwancundiire haali.
2.
Haihi n’ebigere bye, kikaro ky’omusiisi,
Ha ndabya ebibi byange, N’obuhumurro bwange
Okusaba kwange kwona, Nkuseesa hali Uwe,
Kandi omu mbabazi ze, Anyikiriza haali.
3.   
Yesu ompe omugisa, Ondole n’onganyira,
Nyimukiz(e) amaiso gaawe, ompe okunihira,
Nyikalege omul(i) Iwe (o)mu kuhikirra kwawe
Ndolerrege Iwe wenka, ampa okuhikirra.
