YESU IWE AYANCUNGWIRE
Original: O Jesus my Redeemer 
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 139
1.
Yesu Iwe ayancungwire, Omuhimbo gwange;
Omujuni ampumuza, (O)mu bujune bwingi.
Chorus
Omucunguzi wange, Ndakuhaisaniza;
Enganjani akira, busah(o)Akwingana.
2.    
Niiw(e) Mpumurro yange, Mukujwaha kwona;
(O)mu mwirima, obunjwaha, Nkasalirwa muno.
3.    
Omujuni nkwesiga, (O)mwebembezi wange;
Onkirra (a)bantu boona, Ab’omu nsi munu.
4.    
Iw(e) Omuhimbo gwange, (O) munsi enu ey’ekibi.
(O) Bunzina nsemererwa, Ntekerez(a) iguru.
