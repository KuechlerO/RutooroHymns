214
NINKUGONZA AI YESU
Original: I know I Love Thee Better, Lord
Composer: Ralph E. Hudson
Key/Doh: C
Luganda: 148
1.
Ninkugonza Ai Yesu okukira byona;
Habwobusinge obunyine, obwikara omunda.
Chorus
Bingi ebitutamanyire eby’engonzi zawe;
Bingi ebitutamanyire (e)by’esagaama yaawe.
2.    
Nimanya onyikara haihi, okukira boona;
(O)bunteekereza haliwe, nsemererwa muno.
3.   
Okampa okusemererwa, n’omuhimbo mwingi;
Habw’engonzi zaawe enyingi, bujune bugenda.
4.    
Iw(e) Omujuni oungonza, ndigambaki owawe;
Hat(i) obundaaba nindora omuhimbo mwingi?
