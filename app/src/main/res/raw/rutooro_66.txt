66
MUMUHURRE OMULIISA
Original: Hark! Tis the Shepherd’s Voice
Composer: William A. Ogden
Luganda: 194
1.
Mumuhurre Omuliisa, Nayeta entaama (e)ziri;
Omw’irungu ly’omwirima, Ha zahabiire omu nsi.
Chorus
Zireete Zireete, Oziihe omu nsi y’ekibi;
Zireete, Zireete, Ozireete hali Yesu.
2.
Aramukonyera nooha, Okuserr(a) ezibuzire?
(O)kuzigarr(a) omw’isibiko, Haziikara n’obusinge.
3.
Nizijugir(a) omw’irungu, Kandi na ha nsozi zoona;
Mukama nakugamba ngu, Habura Entaama zange.
