192
MU KUSEMERERWA N’OKUSIIMA
Original: In Joyful High
Luganda: 234
1.
N’okusemerewa kwingi ninyimuky(a) iraka lyaange;
Ndazinanta ha kugonza kwa Yesu okw’amahano?
Chorus
Ngonzi zaawe za mahano, Ngonzi zaawe Yesu
Ngonzi zaawe za mahano, Ngonzi zaawe Yesu.
2.   
Mu muyaga, mu mwirima, nyamusana rund(i) ekiro;
Mu bujune, n’omu kufa ndazinaga kugonza kwe.
3.   
Bu ndasabag(a) onganyire, bu ndagwaga Onyimukye;
Mu bwomeezi n’omu kufa ndazina kugonza kwe.
