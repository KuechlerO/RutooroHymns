42
NYIJA HA MUSALABA
Original: I am coming to the Cross
Composer: W. McDonald
Luganda: 15
1.    
Nyija ha Musalaba, Nyow(e) Omusiisi mali;
Eby’ensi kambiferwe, Ningonza okujunwa.
Chorus
Ninkwesiga Mukama, Iw(e) Omwana gw’entaama;
(A)yafire habw(a) ababi, Ncungura ai Yesu.
2.    
Ndaikara na Yesu, Baitu tindi murungi;
(U)we wenka ansemeze, Ek(i) akakinihiza.
3.
Ninkuh(e) ebyange byoona, N’emiganyano nayo;
Omwoyo nomubiri, Byoona bitwale Yesu.
4.
Nyesig(a) okufa kwawe, Nebi (e)biwanize;
Nyijwire ebibi bingi, Nimbireta haliiwe.
5.    
Mukama ninkusaba, Onsemez(e) omutuma;
Onyeze n’esagama, Ohabw(e) ekitinisa.
