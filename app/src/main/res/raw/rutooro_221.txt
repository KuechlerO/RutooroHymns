221
OBWALIIJA MUKAMA WAITU
Original:  When He Cometh
Composer: J. R. Sweney
Key/Doh: E
Luganda: 196
1.
Bwaliija Mukama waitu okututabarra,
Abamulindirra bwona, nibamugonza.
Chorus
Nkenyunyuzi z’omu mwanya okuzaaka ha’iguru;
Nabo nukwo balisana bagonzibwa be.
2.   
Alisoroleza haali, abaanabe boona,
Abataho omutuma okumuserra.
3.   
Abaguda nabanaku abagonza Yesu,
Baliheebw(a) empera yabu (o)bwaligaruka.
4.   
Boona Abagonza Yesu alibajuna,
Nk’amabale g’omuhendo omwingi muno.
