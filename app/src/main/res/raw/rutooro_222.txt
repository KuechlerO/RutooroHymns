NZOIRE ENGANJANI 
Original:  I’ve Found a Friend
Composer: G. C. Stebbins
Key/Doh: A
Luganda: 49
1.
Nzoir(e) Enganjani yange, ayabandiz(e) okungonza,
Akancunda n’engonzize zangumiz(a) Omuluwe;
Ali mu mutuma gwange nkensoro z’obwomeezi,
Yesu wange naanye ndi we, ebiro byona Amiina.
2.   
Nzoir(e) Enganjani yange, (a)yangondeze yanfeera,
Akanyeha n’obwomeezi, obwebiro n’ebiro;
Byoona nuwe (a)yabimpaire, Nyowe ndi mukeeto,
Eby’itungo n’obwomeezi, hibye ebiro byoona.
3.   
Nzoir(e) enganjani yang, Yesu Ow’amaani goona,
Alintaahya n’obusinge owaitu Omwiguru;
Nyijwiire omuniiniiro, Ndengiire (e)byomw’iguru,
Kanyekambe ndwaaane njwaahe ndihumurra mw’iguru.
4.   
Nzoir(e) Enganjani yange, (o)w’embabaz(i) omwesigwa,
Mwebembezi wa’magezi andwanirra butoosa;
Ndaatiinaki obwaloho? Nuwe Mulinzi wange,
Mubwomeezi n’omu kufa, Tintiina kabi koona.
