115
MUKAMA MWANDARA GWAITU
Original: The Lord’s our Rock
Composer: F. E. Belden
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 20
1.   
Mukama mwandara gwaitu, Leka twebingege haali;
Nuw(e) atukiza (o)mu kabi, Nuwe bwirukiro bwaitu.
Chorus
Omwandara ogw’amaani, (O)bwesereko bw’abanaku;
(O)mwebembezi w’abagenzi, Omwebingw(a) omu bujune.
2.    
Nuw(e) atukiza (o)mu byona, Leka twebingege haali;
Titutinisibw(a) omubi, Nuwe bwirukiro bwaitu.
3.    
(O)bujune (o)bubutwizira, Leka twebingege haali;
Nuwe obusinge bwaitu, Nuwe bwirukiro bwaitu.
4.    
Iwe (O)mwandara gw’abantu, Ntwirukira hali Iwe;
Otukonyerege bwona, Niiwe bwirukiro bwaitu.
