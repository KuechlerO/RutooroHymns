29
YESU AYANGONDOZE
Original: Jesus Lover of My Soul
Composer: S. B. Marsh
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 30
1.   
Yesu ayangondeze, Ninyija namber(e) oli;
Ebigonzi by’omu nsi, nibinjwahiza kimu;
Nserek(a) omu mikono, abanyanzigwa baingi;
Tinyina (o)bwirukiro, Otwal(e) omwoyo gwange.
2.   
Busaho obwirukiro, Okwihaho hal(i) Iwe;
Otansiga nduhire, Ndi haihi n’okufa;
(O)kusemererwa kwange, Kuruga namber(e) oli;
Ntaahye omutwe gwange, Omu mikono yaawe.
3.   
Niiwe wenka nyesiga, Busaho akwingana;
Agwire omwiimukye, Tambira omuceke;
Omurwaire (o)mukize, Igura amaiso ge;
Nyowe nyina ebibi, Iwe ohikiriire.
4.   
Mpa embabazi zaawe, Ragaz(a) ebibi byange;
Nduhiriire ndetera, amaizi g’obwomeezi;
Ensoro z’obwomeezi, Kantahe omu honka;
(O)mwoyo gwange guijule, Amaizi g’obwomeezi.
