73
OMANYISE EBYA YESU
Original: More About Jesus
Composer: J. R. Sweney
Luganda: 54
1.   
Omanyise ebyaYesu, Nyoleka okugonza kwe;
Nuwe Omujuni wange, Ayangondeze yanfera.
Chorus
Kambaze haYesu, Kambaze haYesu;
Kambaze ha kugonzakwe, Nuwe Omujuni wange.
2.   
Omanyise ebya Yesu, Nyoleka okugonza kwe;
Onyegesege bwona, Okukora okurungi.
3.   
Omanyise ebya Yesu, Kandi leka mbihurrege;
Okunihiza kwon(a) okwa-Nyina omu kitabu kye.
4.  
Omanyise ebya Yesu, Hati ali rubajuki?
Oyongere onyegese, Ndihika nta namber(e) ali?
