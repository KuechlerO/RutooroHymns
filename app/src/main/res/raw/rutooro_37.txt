37
ANTEREKEREZA YESU
Original: He Leadeth Me
Composer: William B. Bradbury
Key/Doh: D
Luganda: 151
1.   
Anterekereza Yesu, Omuhimbo gunkwasire;
Angumya n’omukono gwe, Yesu kunterekereza.
Chorus
Angumya n’omukono gwe, Yesu nuwe mukonyezi;
Ija ij(a) mu mutuma, Ija (o)mu mutuma gwange.
2.   
Obumba nyin(a) obujune, Yesu nuwe mukonyezi;
(O)mu businge n’obujune, Angumya n’omukono gwe.
3.   
Mukama kanyije bwango, Hati kanyanguhe bwango;
(O)bugumizo bwange bwona, Yesu nuwe mukonyezi.
4.   
Obutulihika oku, (o)mw’ibara lye tulyesima;
Tuhikire namber(e) ali, TulimukugizaYesu.
