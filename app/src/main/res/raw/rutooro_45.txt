45
NKWETAAGA AI MUKAMA
Original: Blessed Lord, How much I need Thee
Composer: F. E. Beldon
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 16
1.  
Nkwetaaga Ai Mukama, Nimpamanga ndi Musiisi;
Oije onyebembere, Onkwatirre ntagwa.
Chorus
Omu saaha enu, Nkwetaaga Ai Mukama;
Omu saaha enu, Ninkusab(a) otansiga.
2.   
Onjweke obuculeezi, Onjule emyehembo;
Sukur(a) obugara bwange, Nyiriza haihi Naiwe.
3.   
(O)muliiwe mba n’obusinge, Setani arankoraki?
(O)ndinda mu rugendo rwange, (O)li kunihira kwange.
4.   
Byona ebirambahoga, Buguuda n’okwinara;
Ndagumiraga haliiwe, (O)bwigamiro bw’amaani.
