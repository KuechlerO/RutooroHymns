149
NDI MUJASI W’OMUSALABA
Original: A Soldier of the Cross.
1.
Ndi Mujasi w’omusalaba, Yesu hayanferiire;
Ndaikaraga nimuhondera, Ninkora eby’agonza.
Chorus
(O)mw’ibara ly’omuhendo, Ndigumira enaku
Habw’embabazize enyingi, Alinjweka ekondo.
2.
Nditwarwa omu Iguru, Omu kusemererwa;
Abo abatwarwa eby’omu nsi, Balyegomb(a) Iguru.
3.
Omu bwire bw’omunyanzigwa, (E)mbabazi ze zinjune;
Omu nsi enu ey’ekibi, Anyogy(e) omu sagamaye.
4.
Ndirwana nukwo nsingule, Mukam(a) omp(e) amaani;
Ongumye mbege mwesigwa, Mpurrege Iraka lyawe.
