BUSAHO IBARA NKE RYA YESU
Original: There’s No Other Name like Jesus
Composer: F. Edson Belden
1.
Busaho Ibara nk’erya Yesu; Ly’omuhendo Ly’amaani,
Ly’omuhimbo omu Iguru, Ly’omuhimbo n’omunsi
Chorus
Lirungi, Lirungi, busaho ibara nk’erya Yesu,
Lirungi, Lirungi, busaho ibara nk’erya Yesu.
2.
Busaho ibara nk’erya Yesu, Obujune obubwija,
Busaho ibara nk’eryaYesu, Omuhimbo obugwija.
3.
Nukwo kunihira kwange, ndimurora obu aliija,
Ndimutangirra obu aliija, mpumule omu mutima.
4.
Obu aracwamuga ngende, okutebez(a) abandi,
Nimusaba ankonyerege n’emigisaye enyingi.
5.
Obu aracwamu ampumuze, ankwate ha mukono,
Ndisiima Yesu wange, mpumuliire omul(i) Uwe.
