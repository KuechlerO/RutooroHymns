17
IZOOBA LY’OMWOYO GWANGE
Original: Sun of My Soul
Composer: John Keble
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 93
1.   
Izooba ly’omwoyo gwange, Omujuni oungonza,
Obwaba nayangasana, (E)kiro kifook(a) omusana.
2.   
Emitalibano (e)y’ensi, Ereke kunsikiriza,
(O)musana gw’amaiso gaawe; N’okuntanga (o)kumurora.
3.   
(O)bungenda (o)kubyam(a) ekiro, Ondinde (o)mu bubi bwona,
(O)nkwat(e) omu mikono yaawe, ompumuze nobusinge
4.   
Nyenkya, ihangwe n’ekiro, N’omubiikaro byona,
(E)nganjani y’abantu boona, Ikara hamu na nyowe.
5.   
Rund(i) omu baana baawe, Nagay(a) embabazi zaawe,
(O)mujune omwana wawe, Omu maani ga Setani.
