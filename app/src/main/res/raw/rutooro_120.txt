RUHANGA AIKALEGE NAINYWE
Original: God be with you
Composer: W.G. Tomer
Key/Doh: Db
Luganda: 50
1.
Ruhanga aikalege nainywe, Abahege omugisa
Abalinde (o)mu kugonza Okuhikya Yesu (o)bwaliija.
Chorus
Murame, Murame, Mugende n’obusinge;
Murame, murame, Omujuni aikale nainywe.
2.
Ruhanga aikalege nainywe, Abakwate ha mukono,
Abaliise eby’omwoyo, abalinde (o)mu kugonza kwe.
3.
Ruhanga aikalege nainywe, (O)bumulizirwa kutiina,
(E)mbabazi ze zibasembe, Abege nainywe buli kiro.
4.
Ruhanga aikalege nainywe kandi abah(e) Obusinge,
Abarabye (o)mu bigonzi, abahikye omu businge.
