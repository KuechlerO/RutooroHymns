47 
EMIGISA YAAWE MUKAMA
Original: Lord I Hear of Showers of Blessing
Composer: W. B. Bradbury
Key/Doh: G
Luganda: 39
1.   
(E)migisa yaawe Mukama Mpuliire ogigaba;
Abandi(o)bubagitunga nanyowe empikeho.
Chorus
Ndi, hanu, Ndi hanu, Nanyowe empikeho.
2.
Yesu Mukam(a) otansiga Dor(a) okundi omubi;
Ncungura (o)mu bibi byona, Mpa embabazi zaawe.
3.   
N’obundaba nkusobeza, N’okukubihiza Iwe;
Obunkuratira eby’ensi, Ninkusaba onganyira.
4.
Ai Mukama (o)nganyire, Onyikirize hooli;
Ompe obusinge bwawe, N’embabazi zaYesu.
