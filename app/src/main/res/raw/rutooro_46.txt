46
AI YESU TWIZIRE
Original: Father We Come to Thee
Composer: Jane Borthwick
Key/Doh: F
Luganda: 21
1.   
Ai Yesu twizire, Tiharoho ondi, Twebingire Iwe, Ntukusaba;
Tuli (o)mu mwirimaYesu (o)bwotaroho, Otukyanganukye, ai Yesu.
Chorus
Yesu tuli hanu, Twikirize;
Busah(o) ondi weena, Otuhurre.
2.
Banyanzigwa baitu, N’emyehembo y’ensi,
Tukirr(e) omuliiwe, Otulinde;
Tuli abatiini, Otuh(e) obumanzi, Tuteege amaani, Tusingule.
3.   
Tujwek(e) embabazi, Tube (a)baana baawe,
Tukize ebibi, Otusembe;
Tugumize hooli, Tusinguze ensi, Otukwatirr(e) itwe, Okutagwa.
