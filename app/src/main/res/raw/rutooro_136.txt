136
NABIREGE OMU NGARO
Original: I washed My Hands
Composer: J. N. H. Risecrane
1.
Nabireg(e) omu ngaro, Zayera tititi;
Nsobole kumukorra Yesu Omujuni.
Chorus
Ebigere byange wegendereze,
Nkozes(a) ebi byonka Yesu (e)byagonza.
2.    
Kandi n’amatu gange, Gahurrege byona;
Byona ebinsemerra, Nukwo mbikolege.
3.    
Kandi n’amaiso gange, Gaterekerrege;
Ningonza kugalema nkorrege Mukama,
4.    
Ningonza orulimi rube rwa Mukama,
Mbazeege buli kiro ebimusemeza.
5.    
Ndarolerraga Uwe, (O)mu byon(a) ebinkora;
Nganyirw(e) ebibi byona omu ibara lya Yesu.
