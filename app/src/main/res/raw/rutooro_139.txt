139
HURRA ENSIMBI
Original: Hear the Pennies Dropping
Composer: William J. Kirkpatrick
1.
Ensimbi nizigwa, Ozihuliire?
Zigwa (o)mu nsaho, Ey’ebisembo.
Chorus 
Hurr(a) ensimbi omu nsaho nizikoigana;
Tuziha Mukama, Atwale zoona.
2.
Tumuhege bwna itwena abato,
Dora ekisembo, kyaitu abato.
3.
Yesu itw(e) abato Tuhaire kike,
(O)butulikura tulikiz(a) aho.
4.
N’obutwina bike Tukugondeze,
Kihongwa kyaitu, Ekyembababazi.
