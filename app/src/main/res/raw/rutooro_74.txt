74
 NSEMERERWA OKUGAMBA
Original: I Love to Tell the Story
Composer: W. G. Fischer
Luganda: 53
1.   
Nsemererwa okugamba ha by’omu Iguru,
(E)kitinisa kya Yesu kandi (o)kwatugonza;
Ningonz(a) okubigamba habw’okubigumya,
Bimpumuz(a) omutuma okukira byona.
Chorus
Nsemererwa (o)kugamba ha bigambo ebi,
Ebigamb(o) ebikuru (E) mbabazi za Yesu.
2.   
Ningonza okutebeza (E)byamahano mali,
(E)bikira byon(a) eby’ensi Ebintekereza;
Ndabazaaga ebi bikuru hali itwe,
Habw’eki nsemererwa (O)kubigamba hoona.
3.   
Binsemez(a) omutuma Okubigambaho,
Bwona (o)bumbigambaho bireet(a) omuhimbo;
Binsemez(a) okugamba baingi tibamanya,
Okujuna kwa Yesu omu kigambo kye.
