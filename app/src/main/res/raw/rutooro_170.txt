170
NGUMIZEMU NINDUBATA
Original: I am Pressing On
Composer: J. Oatman Jr.
1.
Ngumizemu nindubata, nyeyongera buli kiro;
Kandi nsaba (o)mu rugendo, Mukama ngumya n’amaani.
Chorus
Onyimukye nukwo ngume, Ndolerr(e) oku omu Iguru;
Mu bwomeezi oburungi, Mukama ngumya n’amaani.
2.
Omu nsi enu ntamirwemu, mbere hali okutiina;
Abasiisi bagigonza, nyowe nsaba amaanige.
3.
Ninyegomba okwij(a) oku, n’obundaba ninkyacanda;
Kanimpurra omuhimbo, n’ebizina eby’omwiguru.
4.
Kaningonza nyeyongere, okuror(a) ekitinisa;
Kyonka nsaba nukwo ntaahe, Onyebembere mpikeyo.
