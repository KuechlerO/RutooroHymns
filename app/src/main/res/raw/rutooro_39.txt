39
ABASIISI BOONA OMU NSI 
Original: Chief of Sinners
Composer: W. McComb
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 121
1.   
(A)basiisi boon(a) omu nsi, (O)bubi nyowe mbakira,
Baitu Yesu akaija, Yafa okuncungura;
(E)sagama ye (e)kaseswa, Nukwo ntung(e) obwomeezi.
2.   
Okugonza kwe kwingi, Kuraihire nk’iguru;
(O)kuhama kwakwo kwingi, (E) biro byona kuroho;
Okwanyihire nyowe, (O)mumalembo g’okufa.
3.   
N’obundi omusiisi, Nuwe byona habwange;
N’amanya nyow(e) okundi, (E)mihito yang(e) eba ye;
(O)bumba nawe tintiina, (O)mujuni ngabu yange.
