26
RUHANGA ISIITWE 
Original: Come Thou Almighty
Composer: Friedrich F. Fleming
Luganda: 125
1.   
Ruhanga Isiitwe, Tukuhaisaniza, Tukuramye!
Iw(e) omusinguzi, Owekitinisa,
Tukwirre haihi Iwe (O)muhangi.
2.   
(O)mwana wa Ruhanga, Ogaruke bwango, Ba (o)muliitwe!
Tukol(e) ebyogonza, Tukuherezeege,
Ebiro byona tubege baawe.
3.   
(O)mwoyo Arukwera, Tuli baana baawe, twikirize!
Osobora byona , Oli (o)w’okwesiga,
(A)maani gaawe gatusobozese.
4.   
Ruhang(a) atahwaho, (O)w’ebiro n’ebiro Obusatu;
Omusana gwawe Gwetoroole hoona,
Aba hansi boona bakumanye.
