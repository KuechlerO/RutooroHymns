116
TWOMBEKE HA MWANDARA KRISTO
Original: We’ll Build on the Rock
Composer: F. E. Belden
Key/Doh: Ab
Luganda: 72
1.
Twombeke ha Mwandara Kristo, Omwandara gw’obwomeezi;
Okuhulika n’ekiyaga, Titutiina obubiija.
Chorus
Twombeke haluwe, Twombeke haluwe;
Twombeke ha Mwandara gwaitu, Yesu Kristo wenka.
2.    
(A)bamu bombeka ha musenyi, Ha birooto by’obuguuda;
Abandi nabo nibombeka, Ha miganda y’ekibi.
3.    
(A)bandi bombeka ha nkungani, (E)kitinisa n’emyehembo;
Iwe yombeka ha Mwandara, Yesu Kristo Mujuni.
