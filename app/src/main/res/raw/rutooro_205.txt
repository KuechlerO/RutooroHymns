MUKAMA NYOWE NYINA EBIBI 
Original: Just As l Am
Composer: William B. Brandbury
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 140
1.
Mukama nyowe nyin(e) ebibi, baitu embabazi zaawe;
Onyesir(e) okwija hooli, nk’omusiisi Yesu nyija.
2.    
Yesu Omwana gw’entama, Habw’ebibi byang(e) okafa;
Wasesa Esagama yaawe, Nk’Omusiisi Yesu nyija.
3.    
Anyizira Timubinga Nyikiriz(e) Ekigambokye;
Amananu nug(o) Ogamba, Yesu Nyizir(e) Onganyire.
4.    
(O)kugonza kwawe kwingi, N’embabazi bindesire;
Ningonza okuba Waawe, Nk’omusiisi Yesu nyija.
