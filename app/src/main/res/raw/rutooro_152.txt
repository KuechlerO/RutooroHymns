152
OMUFUMU ALI HAIHI
Original: The Great Physician
Composer: J. Hart Stockton
1.
Omufumu ali haihi, Mukama w’embabazi,
Ahumuza (a)bajwahire, huliriz(a) Iraka lye.
Chorus
Aramibwa (A)baserafi, Naramibwa nab’omu nsi,
Kirungi okuzina ha ibara lyaYesu.
2.   
Hurra okwarukugamba (e)bibi bikuganyirwe,
Gendera omu businge, alikujweka ekondo.
3.   
Ai Omwana gw’entama, nkwikiriza Mujuni,
Nkusemererirwe muno, N’iraka Iy’embabazi.
4.   
Ibara lye nirijuna, Yesu Mujuni wange,
Kinsemeza okuhurra, nibakuramya Yesu.
