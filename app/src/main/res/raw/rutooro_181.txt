TUBIKUHA BYONA BYAWE
Original: We Give Thee But Thine Own
Composer:  W. W. How
Luganda: 95
1.
Tubikuha byona byawe nobubirabaga bingi;
Binu byona ebitwine, bisembo oby’otwahuza.
2.   
Tuh(e) embabazi twikale, (o)baketo baw(e) abesigwa;
Tusoroozege n’omwoyo, ebintu ebitwinabyo.
3.   
Amahanga goon(a) ag’ensi, nigetaaga Omujuni;
Abantu abo (o)kabafera, babuzire basasaine.
4.   
Akasumi kahikire, kugarra abahabire;
Kusera ababuzire, kuhumuza abaferirwe.
5.   
Yesu nukwo yakozire, yayehayo kubakiza;
Naitwe katukole bwona, abasisi bacungurwe.
6.   
Yesu nuwe atwegesa, tubimukorrege byona;
Itwena leka twekambe tugabege tukonyere.
