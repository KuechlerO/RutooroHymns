6
OBUKURU BWA MUKAMA
Original: I sing the Mighty Power
Composer: George Frederick Root
Luganda: 38
1.
Obukuru bwa Mukama (a)yahangire byona;
Niiwe wakozire (e)nyanja ahamu n’omwanya,
Niiwe ojwesa izooba (o)kutumulikira,
(O)kwezi hamu n’enyunyuzi, biragirwa Iwe.
2.
Ninsiim(a) obukuru bwawe otuh(a) eby’okulya;
Okahang(a) ebintu byona wabyeeta birungi,
Buli hantu hanta (a)maiso ndora ebikuru,
(O)buncumitiriza ensi, ahamu n’iguru.
3.
Ebimera rundi (e)byakyo byolek(a) obukuru;
Ekiyaga n’kuhinda nibikworobera,
Obwomeezi bugabwa Iwe, Niiw(e) olema byona,
Kandi buli kintu kyona lwe okirora.
