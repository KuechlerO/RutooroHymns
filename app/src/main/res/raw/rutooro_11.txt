11
YESU IJA NAMBERE NDI
Original: Come Thou Fount of Every Blessing
Composer: A. Nettleton
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 10
1.   
Yesu Ija namberendi, Omu mutuma gwange
Guzinege (e)biro byona, Kukugizaaga Iwe.
Onyegesege nkuramye, Manyiseege (O)kujunwa
N’okunihira iguru, Nibyo binsemezege.
2.
Kanyimuky (e) Iraka lyange, Ninkusab(a) onkonyere,
Okugonza kwaw(e) okwingi, Olimpikya (o)mw’iguru;
Yesu Mucunguzi wange, Ayankiriz(e) okufa,
(A)kanyihaho (e)by’okusiisa, Okasiingur(a) okufa.
3.   
Yesu okugonza kwawe, Kweyongerege muno;
Habw’embabazi enyingi, Kangumirege hooli;
Kandi ntalikurugaho, Nyikalege Naiwe,
(O)mutuma gwange gwikale, Nihirege iguru.
