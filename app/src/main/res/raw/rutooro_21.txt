21
HABW’OKUIKIRIZA  
Original: My Faith Look Up to Thee
Composer: L. Mason
Key/Doh: Eb
Luganda: 123
1.   
Habw’okuikiriza, Nyowe ndikurora, Omujuni;
Hurra ninkusaba, Njun(a) ebibi byona,
Nubwo ndikurora n’obusinge.
2.   
Yesu (o)w’embabazi, Omp(e) amaani gaawe, Ndisingura;
Nk’okuwanferiire, Byona kambikuhe,
Obwomeezi bwange bukukorre.
3.   
Nkonyera Mukama, Okwetegereza Ekitabu;
(O)mwoy(o) amulikire, (O)bundakisomaga,
Omu kitab(u) omu nkurolege.
4.   
Hanu nintuntura, Habw’eki ninyija, namber(e) oli;
Nkwata ha mukono, Ndeke (o)kutuntura,
Ontwale owaawe, Obwoliija.
