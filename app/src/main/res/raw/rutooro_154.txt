154
AYI YESU AMAISO GANGE  
Original: Jesus These Eyes have never Seen
Key/Doh: D
Luganda: 131
1.
Ayi Yesu amaiso gange, Tigakaroraga;
Omwirima n’obugusweka, Tinyin(a) ekindora.
2.   
Tindora kandi timpurra, Iraka lya Yesu;
Onyikale haihi muno, Mu buli kusaba.
3.   
Ensi tin(a) ekikaro, Ky’omuhendo nkeki;
Kikaro eki ndirorramu, Yesu enganjani.
4.   
Nk’ebirooto ebirungi, Ebij(a) omu turo;
Yesu nukwo anyizira, Nintung(a) okwesiima.
